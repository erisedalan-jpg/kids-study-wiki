## 推荐学习路径

### 阶段 1: 过去时强化 (U1-U2, 2 周)
- 一般过去时综合巩固 → 旅行经历语境叙述
- 不规则动词第二组: eat/ate, drink/drank, feel/felt, meet/met
- 频率副词梯度: always/usually/often/sometimes/hardly ever/never
- How often...? 询问频率; How many times a week?

### 阶段 2: 比较级与最高级 (U3-U4, 3 周)
- 形容词比较级构成: -er/more + adj. + than → [[比较级]]
- 不规则比较: good→better→best; bad→worse→worst
- 最高级构成: -est/most + adj. + in/of → [[最高级]]
- 比较句综合: A is taller than B; C is the tallest in...

### 阶段 3: 电视与计划 (U5-U6, 2 周)
- 电视节目词汇: talk show/game show/sitcom/documentary/news
- want to / would like to + 动词原形
- be going to + 动词原形 → 计划/意图表达 → [[053-一般将来时|be going to]]
- 新年决心语境写作

### 阶段 4: will 将来时 (U7, 2 周)
- will + 动词原形 → 预测/承诺 → [[will将来时]]
- will vs be going to 区别 (预测 vs 计划)
- There will be... 将来存在句
- 时间状语: in 5 years / in the future / someday

### 阶段 5: 食谱与邀请 (U8-U9, 2 周)
- 祈使句 + first/then/next/finally 顺序副词 → 食谱写作
- 数量表达 one cup of / two teaspoons of / a little
- 邀请 Can you come to...? / Would you like to...?
- 婉拒 I'm sorry, I can't. I have to...

### 阶段 6: 条件句 + 期末复习 (U10, 2 周)
- if 条件状语从句 → 主句 will + V → [[209-if条件句（一类条件）|if条件句]]
- 真实条件句 (If you..., you will...)
- 全册语法横向梳理; 比较级/最高级/将来时综合

总课时约 18 周 ≈ 1 学期.

## 重点知识点

### 语法点
1. **一般过去时** 不规则动词扩展 (第二批)
2. **频率副词** How often 句型; 频率百分比表达
3. **形容词比较级** 规则/不规则; than 连接
4. **形容词最高级** 规则/不规则; in/of 区别
5. **be going to** 将来时: 计划与意图
6. **will** 将来时: 预测与承诺; will vs be going to
7. **want to / would like to** 接动词原形
8. **if 条件状语从句** 真实条件 (现在时 + will)
9. **顺序副词** first/then/next/after that/finally
10. **情态动词 can/could** 表邀请与婉拒

### 高频词汇 (核心 45 个)
vacation, beach, museum, countryside, exercise, habit, outgoing, serious, athletic, funny, smart, theater, comfortable, expensive, documentary, sitcom, news, plan, resolution, prediction, robot, housework, pollution, dangerous, shake, pour, mix, cut, invitation, refuse, suppose, chance, certainly, probably, maybe, succeed, fail, unless, though, still, already, else, each other, together, instead

### 句型与功能
- 叙述旅行: Did you go to...? How was it? It was amazing!
- 询问频率: How often do you...? I... three times a week.
- 比较性格: She is more outgoing than me.
- 表达计划: I'm going to + V this summer.
- 预测未来: There will be more robots in 20 years.
- 描述步骤: First, cut the bananas. Then, pour in the milk.
- 邀请/婉拒: Can you come? I'm sorry, I have to study.
- 条件表达: If you work hard, you'll pass the exam.

## 易错点 / 难点

- **比较级双写**: big→bigger; hot→hotter (闭音节双写规则)
- **more vs -er**: 二音节以上用 more; 单音节/部分双音节用 -er
- **be going to vs will**: be going to 表已有计划; will 表临时决定/预测
- **if 从句时态**: if 从句用一般现在时, 主句用 will (不用 will will)
- **最高级范围**: in a class/in the world (表范围用 in); of all (用 of)
- **频率副词位置**: 实义动词前; be 动词/助动词后
