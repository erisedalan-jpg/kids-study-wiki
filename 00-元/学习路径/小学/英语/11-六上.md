## 推荐学习路径

按 6 阶段 (一学期约 6 单元 + 2 复习):

### 阶段 1: 问路 + 方向 (Unit 1, 2 周)
- turn left / turn right / go straight / traffic light / bookstore / museum → [[交通出行]]缺口
- How can I get to...? / It's near... / Turn left/right at the...
- 路标与地点名称: post office / cinema / hospital / supermarket

### 阶段 2: 交通工具 + 出行方式 (Unit 2, 2 周)
- on foot / by bus / by bike / by subway / by car / by plane → [[交通出行]]缺口
- How do you come to school? / I come by bike.
- 交通规则: Don't go at red light. / I must pay attention to traffic lights.

### 阶段 3: 将来时计划 (Unit 3, 2 周)
- be going to 深化 — 与五下衔接
- What are you going to do tomorrow? / I'm going to have an art lesson.
- When are you going? / We're going to... — 时间状语

### 阶段 4: Recycle 1 + 兴趣爱好 (Unit 4, 2 周)
- hobby: collect stamps / read books / play chess / do kung fu → [[爱好词汇]]缺口
- What are his/her hobbies? / He/She likes... — 第三人称单数 likes
- Does he/she...? / Yes, he does. / No, she doesn't. — 第三人称疑问

### 阶段 5: 职业 (Unit 5, 2 周)
- doctor / teacher / farmer / engineer / coach / reporter → [[职业词汇]]缺口
- What does he do? / He's a doctor. / Where does he work? / He works in...
- 一般现在时第三人称单数全面巩固

### 阶段 6: 情绪 + 健康建议 (Unit 6, 2 周)
- happy / sad / angry / worried / excited / tired / bored → [[情绪词汇]]缺口
- How do you feel? / I feel... / What's the matter? / You should...
- Recycle 2 综合复习

## 重点知识点

- **词汇**: 地点建筑 (8处), 交通工具/方式 (6种), 职业 (6种), 情绪形容词 (7个), 爱好动词词组
- **句型**: How can I get to...? / What does he/she do? / Does he/she...? / How do you feel?
- **语法点**: be going to 将来时系统掌握; 一般现在时第三人称全面巩固 (does/doesn't + 动原); 情态动词 must
- **语音/拼读**: Let's spell — 字母组合 oa/ow (boat/snow); 谚语 East or west, home is best.

## 易错点 / 难点

- **Does he/she...? — Yes, he does.**: 回答用 does 不用动词原形; "Yes, he likes." 是典型错误 ⭐
- **by bus vs on foot**: 规则不一致; by + 交通工具 (无冠词), on foot (固定搭配)
- **be going to vs will**: 六上要求区分; be going to = 有计划/证据; will = 临时决定/预测
- **职业 + 工作地点搭配**: work in a hospital (医院)/school; work at a... — in/at 介词选用
