## there be 句型 / There Be Structure

**定义**：there be 句型表示"某处存在某物/某人"，be 动词根据主语的单复数变化。

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（小学英语回溯版）

**There be** 句型用来说"某个地方有什么"：

- There **is** a book on the desk.（桌子上**有**一本书。）——单数用 is
- There **are** three books on the desk.（桌子上**有**三本书。）——复数用 are

**规律**：
- 后面是单数/不可数 → **there is**
- 后面是复数 → **there are**

**中文"有"的两种英文说法**：
- **have/has**：表示"拥有"（某人有某物）
- **there is/are**：表示"存在"（某地有某物）

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型变化

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | There is/are + 名词 + 地点 | There is a cat under the sofa. |
| 否定句 | There is/are + not + 名词... | There isn't a cat here. |
| 疑问句 | Is/Are + there + 名词 + ...? | Is there a cat under the sofa? |
| 回答 | Yes, there is/are. / No, there isn't/aren't. | Yes, there is. |

### There is vs There are

| 后面接 | 用哪个 | 例句 |
|--------|--------|------|
| 单数名词 | there is | There is a pen on the desk. |
| 不可数名词 | there is | There is some water in the glass. |
| 复数名词 | there are | There are some books on the shelf. |
| 就近原则（and 连接） | 看最近的名词 | There is a pen and two books. |

### 与 have/has 的区别

| 句型 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| There is/are | 某处存在（存在句） | There is a library in our school. |
| have/has | 某人拥有（领属句） | Our school has a library. |

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. There is a bookcase in the classroom. （教室里有一个书柜。）
2. There are many books on the shelf. （书架上有很多书。）
3. Is there a post office near here? — Yes, there is. （这附近有邮局吗？——有。）
4. There isn't any milk in the fridge. （冰箱里没有牛奶了。）
5. How many students are there in your class? （你们班有多少学生？）
