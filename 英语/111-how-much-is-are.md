## How much is/are...? / ……多少钱？

**句型结构**：How much + is/are + 名词?
**功能**：询问价格；购物场景中使用

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（小学英语回溯版）

**How much** 是"多少"，问价格时用！

**两种形式**：
- How much **is** this/that?（这个/那个多少钱？）——单数
- How much **are** these/those?（这些/那些多少钱？）——复数
- How much **is** the apple?（这个苹果多少钱？）
- How much **are** the apples?（这些苹果多少钱？）

**回答**：
- It's **five yuan**.（5元。）
- They're **two yuan each**.（每个两元。）

**货币单位**：
- yuan（元/人民币）
- dollar（美元）
- pound（英镑）

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型变化

| 情况 | 句型 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 单数物品 | How much is + 单数名词? | How much is the ruler? |
| 复数物品 | How much are + 复数名词? | How much are the apples? |
| 加指示代词 | How much is this/that? | How much is this? |
| 加物主代词 | How much is your...? | How much is your bag? |

### 购物场景对话

**买东西**：
- A: Excuse me. How much is this pen?
- B: It's 5 yuan.
- A: I'll take it. / That's too expensive.

**询问总价**：
- How much is that in total?（总共多少钱？）
- How much do I owe you?（我应该付您多少钱？）

### 相关表达

| 场景 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 嫌贵 | That's too expensive! |
| 嫌便宜 | What a bargain! |
| 买了 | I'll take it. / I'll have it. |
| 不买 | No, thanks. / Never mind. |

### How much vs How many
- **How much**：问不可数名词数量 / 问价格
- **How many**：问可数名词数量
  - How many apples do you need?（你需要几个苹果？）
  - How much juice do you want?（你想要多少果汁？）

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. How much is this pencil? — It's two yuan. （这支铅笔多少钱？——两元。）
2. How much are these apples? — They're three yuan each. （这些苹果多少钱？——每个三元。）
3. Excuse me, how much is the schoolbag? — It's 50 yuan. （打扰一下，这个书包多少钱？——50元。）
4. How much is a hamburger at this restaurant? （这家餐厅的汉堡多少钱？）
5. How much are those books in the window? （橱窗里那些书多少钱？）
