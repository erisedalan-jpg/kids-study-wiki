## Is/Are this/that...? / 这/那是……吗？

**句型结构**：Is/Are + this/that/these/those + 名词?
**功能**：询问眼前物体是什么；确认物品身份

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（小学英语回溯版）

**问眼前物体是什么**，有两种方式：

**A. 问"是不是"（Yes/No）**：
- **Is this** a pen?（这是钢笔吗？）→ Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.
- **Is that** your book?（那是你的书吗？）
- **Are these** your pencils?（这些是你的铅笔吗？）

**B. 问"是什么"（Wh-question）**：
- **What is this?**（这是什么？）→ It's a pen.
- **What is that?**（那是什么？）→ It's a ruler.

**近 vs 远**：
- this / these = **近**（在手边）
- that / those = **远**（在远处）

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型汇总

| 句型 | 例句 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| Is this a/an...? | Is this a pencil? | Yes, it is. / No, it isn't. |
| Is that a/an...? | Is that a ruler? | Yes, it is. / No, it isn't. |
| Are these...? | Are these apples? | Yes, they are. / No, they aren't. |
| Are those...? | Are those your books? | Yes, they are. |
| What is this? | What is this? | It's a dictionary. |
| What are these? | What are these? | They're erasers. |

### 回答中的代词替换
- Is **this** a pen? → Yes, **it** is.（用 it 代替 this/that）
- Are **these** apples? → Yes, **they** are.（用 they 代替 these/those）

### 加所有格的变体
- Is this **your** pen?（加物主代词）
- Is this **Tom's** bag?（加 's 所有格）
- Whose is this?（这是谁的？）

### 颜色/形容词描述
- Is this pen **red** or **blue**?（这支笔是红的还是蓝的？）
- Are those **big** or **small** apples?（那些是大苹果还是小苹果？）

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. Is this a pencil? — Yes, it is. （这是铅笔吗？——是的。）
2. Is that your schoolbag? — No, it isn't. It's hers. （那是你的书包吗？——不是，是她的。）
3. What is this? — It's an eraser. （这是什么？——是一块橡皮。）
4. Are these apples? — Yes, they are. （这些是苹果吗？——是的。）
5. Is that a dictionary? — No, it isn't. It's a notebook. （那是字典吗？——不是，是笔记本。）
