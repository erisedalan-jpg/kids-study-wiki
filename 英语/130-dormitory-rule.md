## 宿舍规则 / Dormitory Rule

**音标**：/ˈdɔːmɪtri ruːl/
**词性**：名词短语（可数）
**释义**：宿舍规则，住校生在宿舍中必须遵守的规定

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**Dormitory** 就是"宿舍"（寄宿学校的住宿地方），**dormitory rule** 就是"宿舍规则"。

很多中学生住校（live at school），宿舍里也有很多规定！

**常见宿舍规则**：

| 规则（中文） | 英文表达 |
|-------------|----------|
| 晚上10点熄灯 | Lights out at 10 pm. |
| 不能带食物进宿舍 | No food in the dormitory. |
| 必须保持整洁 | Keep the dorm clean and tidy. |
| 不能大声说话 | Don't make noise. |
| 必须按时起床 | You must get up on time. |

**常见句子**：
- What are the **dormitory rules**?（宿舍规则是什么？）
- You **must** go to bed at 10 pm.（你必须晚上10点睡觉。）
- You **can't** play music loudly at night.（晚上不能大声播放音乐。）

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 词汇拓展

| 词 | 词性 | 释义 |
|----|------|------|
| dormitory | n. | 宿舍（较正式）|
| dorm | n. | 宿舍（口语缩写）|
| boarding school | n. | 寄宿学校 |
| curfew | n. | 宵禁时间（熄灯时间） |
| tidy | adj. | 整洁的 |
| bunk bed | n. | 上下铺 |
| roommate | n. | 室友 |

### 完整规则列举练习

学习用 must / mustn't / can / can't 列举规则：

```
Dormitory Rules:
1. You must keep your room tidy.
2. You must go to bed by 10 pm.
3. You mustn't make noise after 9 pm.
4. You can't bring food into the dorm.
5. You can watch TV until 9 pm.
```

### 常用句型

- **What are the dorm rules in your school?**
- In our dorm, we **have to** be in bed by 10 pm.
- **Lights out** at 10 pm is a strict rule.（10点熄灯是一条严格规定。）

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. The dormitory rules are very strict.（宿舍规则非常严格。）
2. We must keep our dormitory clean and tidy.（我们必须保持宿舍干净整洁。）
3. Students can't make noise after 10 pm.（晚上10点后学生不能制造噪音。）
4. You mustn't bring food into the dormitory.（你不能把食物带进宿舍。）
5. Following dormitory rules helps everyone sleep well.（遵守宿舍规则帮助每个人睡好觉。）
