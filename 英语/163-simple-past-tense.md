## 一般过去时（入门）/ Simple Past Tense

**定义**：一般过去时表示过去已经发生的动作或存在的状态。

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**一般过去时**用来说**过去发生**的事情。

**be 动词的过去时**：
- am/is → **was**
- are → **were**

例子：
- I **was** at home yesterday.（我昨天在家。）
- We **were** happy last week.（我们上周很开心。）

**实义动词的过去时（规则变化）**：
- 一般加 -ed：play → **played**，watch → **watched**
- 结尾有 e 加 -d：like → **liked**，dance → **danced**
- 辅音+y → 去 y 加 -ied：study → **studied**，cry → **cried**
- 重读闭音节双写辅音：stop → **stopped**

**时间标志词**：
- **yesterday**（昨天）
- **last week/month/year**（上周/月/年）
- **ago**（……之前）：two days ago
- **just now**（刚才）

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型变化

**肯定句**：Subject + V过去式 + ...
- I **watched** TV last night.

**否定句**：Subject + did not (didn't) + V原形 + ...
- I **didn't watch** TV last night.

**疑问句**：Did + Subject + V原形 + ...?
- **Did** you watch TV last night?
- Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.

### 常见不规则动词过去式（七下常考）

| 原形 | 过去式 | 中文 |
|------|--------|------|
| go | went | 去 |
| have | had | 有；吃 |
| do | did | 做 |
| see | saw | 看见 |
| get | got | 得到 |
| buy | bought | 买 |
| eat | ate | 吃 |
| come | came | 来 |
| take | took | 拿；乘坐 |
| make | made | 制作 |

### 常用句型（Unit 12 话题）

- **What did you do last weekend?**（上周末你做了什么？）
- **How was your weekend?** — It was great!（你的周末怎么样？——太棒了！）
- **Did you have a good time?** — Yes, I did.

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. I went to the park last Sunday.（上周日我去了公园。）
2. We watched a movie yesterday evening.（我们昨晚看了一部电影。）
3. Did you have breakfast this morning? Yes, I did.（你今早吃早餐了吗？是的。）
4. She didn't go to school yesterday because she was sick.（她昨天没去学校，因为她生病了。）
5. Last weekend, I visited my grandparents and had a great time.（上周末我去看望了祖父母，玩得很开心。）
