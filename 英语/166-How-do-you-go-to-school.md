## 你怎么去上学？/ How Do You Go to School?

**核心句型**：询问出行方式

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**How do you go to school?** 就是"你怎么去学校？"——问别人上学用什么交通方式。

**问答对话**：

| 问 | 答 |
|----|-----|
| How do you go to school? | I take a bus.（我坐公共汽车。）|
| | I ride a bike.（我骑自行车。）|
| | I walk to school.（我走路上学。）|
| | I go by car.（我坐汽车去。）|
| | I take the subway.（我坐地铁。）|

**也可以问**：
- **How do you get to school?**（你怎么到学校？）
- **How does she go to school?**（她怎么上学？）

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型变化（换人称）

| 问题 | 回答 |
|------|------|
| How do **you** go to school? | I take a bus. |
| How does **he/she** go to school? | She rides a bike. |
| How does **Tom** go to school? | He walks. |

### 问时间/距离的扩展

- **How long does it take?** — It takes 20 minutes.（要多长时间？——需要20分钟。）
- **How far is your school?** — It's about 3 kilometers away.（你的学校有多远？——大约3公里。）

### 完整对话示例

```
A: How do you go to school every day?
B: I usually take the bus. Sometimes I ride my bike.
A: How long does it take by bus?
B: About 15 minutes. What about you?
A: I walk. My school is very close to my home.
```

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. How do you go to school? I take a bus.（你怎么上学？我坐公共汽车。）
2. She goes to school by bike every day.（她每天骑自行车上学。）
3. How does your father go to work? He drives a car.（你父亲怎么上班？他开车。）
4. I walk to school because it takes only ten minutes.（我走路上学，因为只需要十分钟。）
5. How do most students in your class get to school?（你们班大多数学生怎么上学？）
