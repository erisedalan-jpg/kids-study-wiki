## 你的周末怎么样？/ How Was Your Weekend?

**核心句型**：评价过去的经历

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**How was your weekend?** 就是"你的周末怎么样？"——问别人周末过得好不好。

**关键词**：was（是 is/am 的过去式）

**常用回答**：

| 回答 | 中文 |
|------|------|
| It was great!（= fantastic / wonderful）| 太棒了！|
| It was good. | 挺好的。|
| It was okay / all right. | 还可以。|
| It was boring. | 很无聊。|
| It was terrible. | 糟透了。|
| It was tiring but fun. | 很累但很有趣。|

**完整对话**：
- **How was your weekend?**（你周末怎么样？）
- It was great! I went to the zoo with my family.（太棒了！我和家人去了动物园。）

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### How was...? 句型拓展

| 问题 | 回答 |
|------|------|
| How was **your weekend**? | It was great! |
| How was **the movie**? | It was exciting! |
| How was **your trip**? | It was amazing! |
| How was **the food**? | It was delicious! |
| How was **your test**? | It was difficult. |

### 评价形容词（过去时常用）

| 正面评价 | 负面评价 | 中性评价 |
|----------|----------|----------|
| great 棒极了 | boring 无聊 | okay 还行 |
| wonderful 精彩 | terrible 糟糕 | all right 还可以 |
| exciting 令人兴奋 | tiring 疲惫 | not bad 不错 |
| amazing 惊人 | disappointing 失望 | so-so 马马虎虎 |

### 完整对话示例

```
A: Hi! How was your weekend?
B: It was fantastic! I went to the Great Wall with my family.
A: Really? How was it?
B: It was amazing! The view was beautiful. 
   How was your weekend?
A: It was boring. I just stayed home and did homework.
B: Oh, that sounds tough. Maybe we can do something fun next weekend?
```

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. How was your weekend? It was great — I went to a concert!（你周末怎么样？很棒——我去看了演唱会！）
2. How was the test? It was very difficult.（考试怎么样？非常难。）
3. How was the food at the new restaurant? It was delicious!（新餐厅的食物怎么样？很美味！）
4. My weekend was boring because I had a lot of homework.（我的周末很无聊，因为我有很多作业。）
5. The trip to Beijing was wonderful — we visited many famous places.（北京之行很精彩——我们参观了很多著名景点。）
