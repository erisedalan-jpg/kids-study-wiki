## 你在做什么？/ What Are You Doing?

**核心句型**：询问当前正在进行的活动（现在进行时）

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**What are you doing?** 就是"你在做什么？"——问别人现在正在做什么。

**问答规律**：

| 问 | 答 |
|----|-----|
| What are you doing? | I'm reading a book.（我正在读书。）|
| | I'm watching TV.（我正在看电视。）|
| | I'm doing my homework.（我正在做作业。）|
| | I'm eating lunch.（我正在吃午饭。）|

**换人称问法**：
- What **is** he/she **doing**?（他/她在做什么？）
- What **are** they **doing**?（他们在做什么？）

**答法结构**：I'm / He's / She's / They're + **动词-ing**

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型变化

| 问题 | 回答 |
|------|------|
| What **are you** doing? | I**'m** studying. |
| What **is he/she** doing? | He**'s** / She**'s** sleeping. |
| What **are they** doing? | They**'re** playing outside. |
| What **are you** doing now? | I**'m** watching a video. |

### 电话情景对话（常考！）

```
(Phone call)
A: Hello! 
B: Hi! What are you doing?
A: I'm watching a movie. What about you?
B: I'm just doing homework. 
A: That's boring. Do you want to come over?
```

### 现在进行时常见动词 -ing 形式

| 原形 | ing 形式 | 规则 |
|------|----------|------|
| read | reading | 直接加 |
| watch | watching | 直接加 |
| swim | swimming | 双写 m |
| run | running | 双写 n |
| write | writing | 去 e 加 |
| dance | dancing | 去 e 加 |

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. What are you doing? I'm doing my homework.（你在做什么？我正在做作业。）
2. Look! What is that dog doing? It's chasing a cat!（看！那只狗在做什么？它在追猫！）
3. What are the students doing now? They're listening to the teacher.（学生们现在在做什么？他们正在听老师讲课。）
4. What is your mother doing? She's cooking dinner in the kitchen.（你妈妈在做什么？她正在厨房做晚饭。）
5. What are you doing this afternoon? I'm going to the library.（你今天下午做什么？我要去图书馆。）
