## 上个周末我…… / Last Weekend, I...

**核心句型**：用一般过去时叙述过去发生的事

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**Last weekend, I...** 就是"上个周末，我……"——讲述上周末做了什么事。

**关键点**：说过去的事用**一般过去时**——动词要变形！

**常见动词变化**：
- go → **went**（去）
- watch → **watched**（看）
- play → **played**（玩）
- visit → **visited**（拜访）
- have → **had**（有；吃）
- see → **saw**（看见）

**常见句子**：
- Last weekend, I **went** to the park.（上周末我去了公园。）
- Last weekend, I **watched** a movie.（上周末我看了一部电影。）
- Last weekend, I **visited** my grandparents.（上周末我去看望了祖父母。）
- Last weekend, I **played** basketball with my friends.（上周末我和朋友打了篮球。）

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 过去时时间词

| 时间词 | 中文 |
|--------|------|
| last weekend | 上个周末 |
| last week | 上周 |
| yesterday | 昨天 |
| yesterday morning | 昨天上午 |
| last night | 昨晚 |
| two days ago | 两天前 |
| last month | 上个月 |
| last year | 去年 |

### 过去时句型变化

**肯定**：I went to the library.
**否定**：I **didn't go** to the library.（didn't + 原形）
**疑问**：**Did** you go to the library?（Did + 主语 + 原形）

### 完整过去时叙述段落

```
Last weekend was great! On Saturday morning, I went to the 
park with my family. We played games and had a picnic. 
In the afternoon, I watched a movie at home. On Sunday, 
I visited my grandparents. We had dumplings for lunch. 
I did my homework in the evening. It was a busy but 
happy weekend!
```

---

## 例句（中英对照）

1. Last weekend, I went to the zoo with my parents.（上周末我和父母去了动物园。）
2. I watched a funny movie last Saturday evening.（上周六晚上我看了一部有趣的电影。）
3. We played basketball in the park last Sunday.（上周日我们在公园打了篮球。）
4. Last weekend, I visited my grandparents and had a great time.（上周末我去看望了祖父母，玩得很开心。）
5. What did you do last weekend? I stayed at home and read books.（你上周末做了什么？我待在家里读书了。）
