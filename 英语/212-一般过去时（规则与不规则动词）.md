# 一般过去时（规则与不规则动词）

## 基本概念

**一般过去时**表示过去某个时间发生的动作或存在的状态。

**标志词**：yesterday, last night/week/year, ago, in 2020, just now...

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 构成方法

**肯定句**：主语 + 动词过去式
- I **visited** the Great Wall yesterday.（规则动词）
- She **went** to Beijing last week.（不规则动词）

**否定句**：主语 + **didn't** + 动词原形
- I **didn't visit** the museum.
- She **didn't go** anywhere.

**疑问句**：**Did** + 主语 + 动词原形 + ?
- **Did** you **go** on vacation? 你去度假了吗？
- **Did** she **visit** her grandparents? 她探望祖父母了吗？

### 规则动词变化

| 类型 | 规则 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般词 | + ed | visit → visited |
| e 结尾 | + d | like → liked |
| 辅音 + y | y → i + ed | study → studied |
| 重读闭音节 | 双写辅音 + ed | stop → stopped |

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### 不规则动词（高频）

| 原形 | 过去式 | 原形 | 过去式 |
|------|--------|------|--------|
| go | went | come | came |
| see | saw | do | did |
| have | had | eat | ate |
| give | gave | take | took |
| make | made | buy | bought |

### 特殊情况：be 动词

| 主语 | 原形 | 过去式 |
|------|------|--------|
| I / he / she / it | am / is | was |
| you / we / they | are | were |

**否定**：was not (wasn't) / were not (weren't)
**疑问**：Was she happy? / Were they at home?

### 常见错误

- ❌ I did went there.（went 已是过去式，不加 did）
- ✅ I went there.
- ❌ She didn't visited the museum.
- ✅ She didn't visit the museum.（否定句用原形）
