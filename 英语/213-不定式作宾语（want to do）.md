# 不定式作宾语（want to do）

## 基本概念

**动词不定式**（to + 动词原形）作宾语，跟在某些动词后面，表示"想做……、打算做……"等含义。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 基本结构

**动词 + to + 动词原形**

- I **want to** be a doctor. 我想当医生。
- She **likes to** read books. 她喜欢读书。
- He **decided to** study harder. 他决定更努力学习。

### 常用"to do"动词

| 动词 | 中文 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| **want to** | 想 | I want to travel. |
| **hope to** | 希望 | She hopes to win. |
| **plan to** | 计划 | We plan to visit Beijing. |
| **decide to** | 决定 | He decided to quit. |
| **need to** | 需要 | I need to rest. |
| **try to** | 尝试 | She tried to help. |
| **learn to** | 学 | He's learning to swim. |
| **like to** | 喜欢 | I like to read. |

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### 不定式 vs. 动名词（-ing）作宾语

不同动词要求不同形式：

| 只接 to do | 只接 -ing | 两者均可（含义不同）|
|-----------|-----------|---------------------|
| want, hope, decide, plan, need, refuse | enjoy, finish, mind, suggest | like, love, hate, start, begin |

示例：
- I **want to** eat pizza.（want 只接 to do）
- I **enjoy eating** pizza.（enjoy 只接 -ing）
- I **like to eat** / **like eating** pizza.（like 两者均可）

### 特殊结构：want sb. to do

**want + 宾语 + to do**：想要某人做某事
- I **want you to** help me. 我想让你帮我。
- She **wants him to** study harder. 她想让他更努力学习。

### 否定形式：not to do

- He decided **not to** go. 他决定不去了。
- She chose **not to** answer. 她选择不回答。
