# How often do you exercise?

## 句型解析

**How often** 是询问频率的疑问词组，意思是"多久……一次"。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 句型结构

**How often + 助动词 + 主语 + 动词原形？**

- How often **do** you **exercise**? 你多长时间锻炼一次？
- How often **does** she **go** swimming? 她多长时间游一次泳？
- How often **did** you **play** tennis last year? 你去年多长时间打一次网球？

### 常见回答

| 回答 | 中文 |
|------|------|
| Every day. | 每天。 |
| Twice a week. | 每周两次。 |
| Three times a month. | 每月三次。 |
| Always / Usually / Often / Sometimes / Hardly ever / Never | 总是/通常/经常/有时/几乎从不/从不 |

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### 回答方式分类

**频率副词**（表示大概频率）：
- I **always** / **usually** / **often** exercise.
- I **sometimes** / **hardly ever** / **never** exercise.

**具体次数**（表示精确频率）：
- **Once** a day / **Twice** a week / **Three times** a month

**具体时间段**：
- **Every day** / **Every weekend** / **Every other day**（每隔一天）

### 扩展问法

- How often do you **go to the gym**?
- How often do you **eat fast food**?
- How often **is** the bus? （公交多久一班？）

### 整合对话（Unit 2 风格）

**A**: How often do you exercise?  
**B**: I exercise every day.  
**A**: What kind of exercise do you do?  
**B**: I usually go jogging in the morning.  
**A**: How long do you jog?  
**B**: About thirty minutes.

---

## 对话示例（中英对照）

| 角色 | 英文 | 中文 |
|------|------|------|
| A | How often do you exercise? | 你多长时间锻炼一次？ |
| B | I exercise twice a week, usually on weekends. | 我每周锻炼两次，通常在周末。 |
| A | What do you do? | 你做什么运动？ |
| B | I go swimming and sometimes play basketball. | 我去游泳，有时打篮球。 |
