# Where did you go on vacation?

## 句型解析

**Where did you go on vacation?**
- **Where**：疑问词，问地点
- **did**：过去时助动词
- **you go**：动词原形（did 后用原形）
- **on vacation**：度假（固定短语）

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

这是八上 Unit 1 的核心句型，用来问别人**假期去哪了**。

### 完整问答模式

**Q**: Where did you go on vacation?  
**A**: I went to + 地点（+ 时间）

| 问 | 答 |
|----|-----|
| Where did you go on vacation? | I went to Hainan. |
| Where did she go? | She went to Beijing. |
| Did he go anywhere? | Yes, he went to Shanghai. / No, he didn't go anywhere. |

### 追问句型

- What did you do there? 你在那里做了什么？
- How was your vacation? 你的假期怎么样？
- Did you have a good time? 你玩得开心吗？

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### 句型结构分析

| 成分 | 词汇 | 功能 |
|------|------|------|
| 疑问词 | Where | 问地点 |
| 助动词 | did | 构成一般过去时疑问 |
| 主语 | you | 被问的人 |
| 谓语 | go | 动词原形（did 后原形）|
| 状语 | on vacation | 说明去的目的/方式 |

### 变体问法

- Where **did** she **go** during the break?
- Where **did** they **travel** last summer?
- Did you **go** anywhere special?
- **Did** you **stay** home or go out?

### 回答的扩展

简答：I went to Yunnan.  
扩展：I went to Yunnan **with my family**. We stayed there **for five days** and visited **many beautiful places**. It was **wonderful**!

## 对话示例（中英对照）

| 角色 | 英文 | 中文 |
|------|------|------|
| A | Where did you go on vacation? | 你去哪里度假了？ |

| A | How was it? | 怎么样？ |

| A | Did anyone go with you? | 有没有人和你一起去？ |
