# Which is the most...?

## 句型解析

用**最高级**进行三者或以上的比较，询问"哪个最……"。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 基本结构

**Which is the most + 形容词, A, B or C?**

- **Which** is **the most popular** sport, basketball, soccer or swimming?  
  篮球、足球和游泳，哪个最受欢迎？

- **Which** is **the most difficult** subject for you?  
  哪门科目对你来说最难？

### 回答

- Basketball is **the most popular**. 篮球最受欢迎。
- For me, math is **the most difficult**. 对我来说，数学最难。

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### 句型对比

| 比较级（两者） | 最高级（三者及以上）|
|--------------|------------------|
| Who is taller, A or B? | Who is the tallest? |
| Which is more popular, A or B? | Which is the most popular? |

### 扩展结构

1. **Which + is/are + the + 最高级 + 名词？**
   - **Which** is **the best** restaurant in town?
   - **Which** team has **the most** players?

2. **范围限定**：
   - the most popular **in the class**
   - the best **of the three**

3. **短词最高级**：
   - Which is **the tallest** building in the world?
   - Which is **the oldest** city in China?

4. **长词最高级**：
   - Which is **the most interesting** movie you've seen?
   - Which is **the most expensive** item in the shop?

## 对话示例（中英对照）

| 角色 | 英文 | 中文 |
|------|------|------|
| A | Which is the most popular subject in your school? | 你们学校哪门课最受欢迎？ |
| B | I think PE is the most popular. | 我认为体育最受欢迎。 |
| A | Which is the most difficult for you? | 哪门课对你最难？ |
| B | Math is definitely the most difficult subject for me. | 数学对我来说绝对是最难的科目。 |
