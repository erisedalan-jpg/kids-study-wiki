# Who is taller, A or B?

## 句型解析

这是用**比较级**进行二选一比较的问句，询问"A 和 B 相比，谁更……"。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 基本结构

**Who is + 比较级, A or B?**  
（A 和 B 谁更……？）

- **Who** is **taller**, Tom or Jerry? 汤姆和杰瑞谁更高？
- **Who** is **funnier**, your dad or your mom? 你爸爸和你妈妈谁更有趣？
- **Which** is **better**, this one or that one? 这个和那个哪个更好？

### 回答方式

- Tom is taller (than Jerry). 汤姆更高（比杰瑞）。
- My dad is funnier. 我爸爸更有趣。
- They are the same height. 他们一样高。

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### Who vs. Which 的选择

| 比较对象 | 疑问词 | 例子 |
|----------|--------|------|
| 人 | Who | Who is taller, A or B? |
| 物 | Which | Which is faster, a car or a bike? |

### 扩展句型

1. **Who + is + 比较级 + 名词, A or B?**
   - Who is **a better singer**, Lisa or Jenny?

2. **Which + is + 比较级, A or B?**
   - Which is **more interesting**, science or history?

3. **A is + 比较级 + than + B**（正面陈述）：
   - Tom is **taller** than Jerry.
   - This book is **more interesting** than that one.

### 追问与对话

**Q**: Who is taller, you or your brother?  
**A**: My brother is taller than me. He's 175 cm, and I'm 168 cm.

**Q**: Which do you like more, swimming or running?  
**A**: I like swimming more. It's more fun and easier on the joints.

---

## 对话示例（中英对照）
