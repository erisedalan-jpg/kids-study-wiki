# argue

## 基本概念

**argue** 是动词，意为"争论、吵架"。名词形式是 **argument**（争论、口角）。

表达：**We often argue about small things.** 我们经常因为小事争吵。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 核心用法

**argue with sb about/over sth** 与某人就某事争论

- They **argued** about who should do the dishes. 他们为谁该洗碗而争论。
- Don't **argue** with your parents. 不要和父母争吵。
- My best friend and I **argued** yesterday. 我和我最好的朋友昨天吵架了。

### 解决争吵

- You should **talk** about the problem. 你们应该谈谈问题所在。
- You should **say sorry** first. 你应该先道歉。
- **Communication** is important in friendship. 沟通在友谊中很重要。

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### argue 的多义

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| argue with sb | 与某人争吵 | Stop arguing with me! |
| argue about/over sth | 就某事争论 | They argued about money. |
| argue for/against | 辩护支持/反对 | He argued for the new plan. |
| argue that... | 主张…（逻辑论证） | She argued that the plan was wrong. |

**argue** 在正式英语中可以表示"提出论据、论证"，不一定是吵架：
- The lawyer **argued** that his client was innocent. 律师论证说他的当事人是无辜的。

### 词族

| 词 | 词性 | 含义 |
|----|------|------|
| argue | v. | 争论 |
| argument | n. | 争论、论据 |
| argumentative | adj. | 好争论的 |
