# fever

## 基本概念

**fever** 是名词，意为"发烧、发热"。体温高于正常值（37°C）时叫 fever。

表达方式：**I have a fever.** 我发烧了。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 核心句型

**I have a fever.** 我发烧了。

相关表达：
- I have a **high** fever. 我高烧。
- I have a **slight** fever. 我有点低烧。
- My temperature is 38.5°C. 我体温 38.5 度。

### 看病时的对话

- A: What's the matter?
- B: I have a fever and a headache.
- A: You should see a doctor right away.
- B: OK. I'll go to the hospital.

### 医生的建议（should / shouldn't）

- You **should** take some medicine. 你应该吃药。
- You **should** drink lots of water. 你应该多喝水。
- You **should** rest at home. 你应该在家休息。
- You **shouldn't** go to school today. 你今天不该去学校。

**I have had a fever since yesterday.** 我从昨天起就发烧了。
