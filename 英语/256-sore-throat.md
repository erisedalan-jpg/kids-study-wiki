# sore throat

## 基本概念

**sore throat** 意为"喉咙痛/嗓子疼"。**sore** 是形容词，意为"疼痛的"；**throat** 是名词，意为"喉咙"。

表达方式：**I have a sore throat.** 我喉咙痛。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 身体不适表达

| 症状 | 英文 |
|------|------|
| 喉咙痛 | a sore throat |
| 头疼 | a headache |
| 发烧 | a fever |
| 咳嗽 | a cough |
| 胃疼 | a stomachache |

**句型**：I have + a/an + 症状名词
- I have **a sore throat**. 我喉咙痛。
- She has **a sore throat** and a cough. 她喉咙痛还咳嗽。

### 医生会说的建议

- You should drink more hot water. 你应该多喝热水。
- You should rest. 你应该休息。
- You shouldn't talk too much. 你不应该说太多话。

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### sore 的其他用法

**sore** 形容词可以修饰多个身体部位：
- a **sore** throat 喉咙痛
- a **sore** back 背部酸痛
- **sore** muscles 肌肉酸痛（运动后）
- **sore** eyes 眼睛酸涩

**sore** 还有"生气的、恼火的"含义（非正式）：
- Don't be **sore** about it. 别为这件事生气。

### 医疗场景对话

- **Doctor**: What's the matter?
- **Patient**: I have a sore throat and a fever.
- **Doctor**: How long have you had these symptoms?
- **Patient**: For two days.
- **Doctor**: You should take this medicine and drink plenty of water.
