# I have already / I haven't...yet.

## 基本概念

**I have already done sth.** 表示"我已经做了某事"（肯定，强调已完成）。
**I haven't done sth yet.** 表示"我还没做某事"（否定，强调尚未完成）。

这是现在完成时中 **already / yet** 的核心句型对。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 句型对比

| 句型 | 意思 |
|------|------|
| I **have already** finished. | 我已经完成了。 |
| I **haven't** finished **yet**. | 我还没完成。 |
| **Have** you finished **yet**? | 你完成了吗？ |

### 家务场景练习

- A: Have you done the dishes yet?
- B: I **have already** done them. Don't worry!

- A: Have you taken out the trash yet?
- B: Not yet. I **haven't taken** it out **yet**.

- A: Has your brother done his homework yet?
- B: Yes, he **has already** done it.

### already 和 yet 的位置

- **already**：放在 have/has 之后（或句末）
  - I have **already** eaten.
- **yet**：放在句末
  - I haven't eaten **yet**.

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### 缩写形式（口语）

- I **have already** → I've **already**
  - I've already finished my homework. 我已经做完作业了。
- I **have not** yet → I **haven't** yet
  - I haven't done it yet. 我还没做。

### 扩展：更多 already / yet 句型

**肯定（already）**：
- She **has already** left. 她已经走了。
- We **have already** seen that movie. 我们已经看过那部电影了。

**否定（yet）**：
- He **hasn't arrived** yet. 他还没到。
- They **haven't decided** yet. 他们还没决定。

**疑问（yet）**：
- **Has** the mail come **yet**? 邮件来了吗？
- **Have** they eaten **yet**? 他们吃了吗？
