# What's the matter? / What's wrong?

## 基本概念

**What's the matter?** 和 **What's wrong?** 都表示"怎么了？出什么问题了？"，用于关心他人或询问症状，是八下 Unit 1 的核心问句。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 两种问法

| 句型 | 语气 |
|------|------|
| **What's the matter?** | 正式、通用 |
| **What's wrong?** | 口语、常用 |
| **What's wrong with you?** | 直接询问 |
| **Are you OK?** | 简单关心 |
| **What happened?** | 发生什么了？ |

### 回答方式

**I have + a/an + 症状名词**（最常用）：
- What's the matter? — **I have a headache.** 我头疼。
- What's the matter? — **I have a fever.** 我发烧了。
- What's the matter? — **I have a sore throat.** 我喉咙痛。
- What's the matter? — **I have a stomachache.** 我胃疼。
- What's the matter? — **I have a toothache.** 我牙疼。

**My + 部位 + hurts/aches**：
- My **back** hurts. 我背疼。
- My **head** aches. 我头疼。

### 对话示例

- A: You look tired. **What's the matter?**
- B: I have a cold. I've been coughing all night.
- A: Oh no! You should see a doctor.
- B: I think I'll rest today.

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### What's the matter with + 人/物

当询问特定的人或物时，后面加 **with**：
- **What's the matter with** Tom? 汤姆怎么了？
- **What's the matter with** this computer? 这台电脑怎么了？

### 类似表达比较

| 表达 | 语境 |
|------|------|
| What's the matter? | 通用，适合看病/关心 |
| What's wrong? | 口语，适合朋友间 |
| What's up? | 极口语，可表示关心也可只是打招呼 |
| Is everything OK? | 较温和的关心 |
| Are you alright? | 英式常用 |

### 建议回应

询问后，通常接 **You should...** 句型：
- What's the matter? — I have a fever.
- You should **see a doctor** right away. 你应该马上去看医生。
- You should **take some medicine**. 你应该吃些药。
- You should **rest at home**. 你应该在家休息。

## 例句（中英对照）

| 英文 | 中文 |

| I have a stomachache. What should I do? | 我肚子疼。我该怎么办？ |
| What's the matter with this phone? It won't turn on. | 这手机怎么了？开不了机。 |
| Nothing's wrong. I'm just tired. | 没什么事，只是累了。 |
