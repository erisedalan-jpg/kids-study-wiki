## 间接引语（疑问句）

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**间接引语疑问句**就是把一个问题"包装"在另一句话里——显得更礼貌、更委婉。

**直接问（生硬）→ 间接问（礼貌）**：

| 直接问 | 间接问（礼貌）|
|--------|------------|
| Where is the restroom? | Could you tell me **where the restroom is**? |
| What time is it? | Do you know **what time it is**? |
| How much does it cost? | Can you tell me **how much it costs**? |

**注意语序变化**：
- 直接疑问句：Where **is** the restroom?（倒装）
- 间接引语：where the restroom **is**（陈述语序，不倒装！）

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 间接引语疑问句的构成

将直接问句嵌入主句中，语序从疑问语序变为陈述语序：

**① 特殊疑问句（wh- 疑问词引导）**：

| 主句 | 引导词 | 从句（陈述语序）|
|------|-------|--------------|
| Could you tell me | where | the restroom **is**? |
| Do you know | what | her name **is**? |
| Can you tell me | when | the train **leaves**? |
| I don't know | why | he **left**. |

**② 一般疑问句（是否型）用 if / whether 引导**：

| 直接问 | 间接问 |
|--------|--------|
| Is he coming? | Do you know **if/whether he is coming**? |
| Does she like music? | I wonder **if/whether she likes music**. |

### 语序变化规则

| 类型 | 直接疑问句 | 间接引语 |
|------|-----------|---------|
| wh- 疑问 | Where **is** she? | where she **is** |
| 一般疑问 | **Is** she there? | if/whether she **is** there |
| 助动词疑问 | What **does** he want? | what he **wants** |

**关键原则**：间接引语从句**恢复主谓正常语序**（主语在前，动词在后）。

### 常用主句

| 主句（礼貌程度递增）| 例句 |
|------------------|------|
| Do you know...? | Do you know where she is? |
| Can you tell me...? | Can you tell me what time it is? |
| Could you tell me...? | Could you tell me where the restroom is? |
| Could you please tell me...? | Could you **please** tell me where I can find a bank? |
| I wonder.../ I'm not sure... | I wonder if she's coming. |

### 时态变化（引导词为过去时时）

主句动词为过去时，从句时态相应回退：
- She **asked** me where I **came** from.（过去时呼应）
- He **wondered** if she **was** coming.

### 中考考点

1. **陈述语序**（非疑问语序）是最高频考点。
2. `if` vs `whether` 引导一般疑问型间接引语（两者均可，but 见 [[宾语从句-初中]] 详细辨析）。
3. 礼貌问路句型：`Could you please tell me + where/when/how...`。
