## Could you please tell me where...?

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**Could you please tell me where...?** 是一句非常礼貌的问路句型，意思是"能告诉我……在哪里吗？"

**与普通问路的区别**：
- 直接问（生硬）：Where is the restroom? 洗手间在哪？
- 礼貌问（温和）：Could you **please** tell me **where the restroom is**? 能告诉我洗手间在哪里吗？

**回答方式**：
- It's on the second floor. 在二楼。
- Go straight and turn left. 直走然后左转。
- Sorry, I'm not sure. 对不起，我不确定。

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型结构分析

```
Could you please tell me + where/when/how/what + 主语 + 动词（陈述语序）?
```

| 成分 | 词 | 功能 |
|------|---|------|
| Could you please | 礼貌请求 | 表示委婉、礼貌 |
| tell me | 主句动词 | 告诉我 |
| where | 引导词 | 引导间接疑问从句 |
| the restroom is | 从句（陈述语序）| 洗手间在哪 |

**关键语序规则**：从句用陈述语序（主语 + 动词），不倒装：
- ✓ Could you tell me where **the restroom is**?
- ✗ Could you tell me where **is the restroom**?

### 礼貌程度对比

| 句型 | 礼貌程度 | 使用场合 |
|------|---------|---------|
| Where is the restroom? | ★★☆ | 熟人、急需时 |
| Do you know where the restroom is? | ★★★ | 一般礼貌 |
| Can you tell me where the restroom is? | ★★★★ | 较礼貌 |
| Could you tell me where the restroom is? | ★★★★★ | 礼貌 |
| Could you **please** tell me where the restroom is? | ★★★★★+ | 最礼貌 |

### 多种问法扩展

| 问的内容 | 句子 |
|---------|------|
| 位置 | Could you please tell me **where** the bank is? |
| 时间 | Could you please tell me **when** the next bus leaves? |
| 方法 | Could you please tell me **how** to get to the station? |
| 价格 | Could you please tell me **how much** this costs? |
| 一般疑问 | Could you please tell me **if/whether** there is a hospital nearby? |

### 中考考点

1. 从句必须用陈述语序（wh- + 主语 + 谓语）。
2. `please` 的位置：放在 tell 前（`please tell me`）或句尾（`tell me, please`）均可。
3. 本句型是 Unit 3 核心，也是间接问句的典型代表。
