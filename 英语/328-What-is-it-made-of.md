## 它是用什么做的 / What is it made of?

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**What is it made of?** 就是"它是用什么做的？"——问某样东西的材料或原料。

**问答练习**：

| 问 | 答 |
|----|-----|
| What is this shirt made of? | It's made of cotton. 棉花。|
| What is this cup made of? | It's made of glass. 玻璃。|
| What is this desk made of? | It's made of wood. 木头。|

**常见材料词汇**：
| 材料 | 英文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 棉花 | cotton | made of cotton |
| 羊毛 | wool | made of wool |
| 丝绸 | silk | made of silk |
| 皮革 | leather | made of leather |
| 玻璃 | glass | made of glass |
| 金属 | metal | made of metal |

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型结构分析

`What is it made of?` = 被动语态疑问句（一般现在时）

| 成分 | 词 | 说明 |
|------|---|------|
| What | 疑问代词 | 询问材料（of 的宾语）|
| is | be 动词（现在时，提前）| 被动语态 |
| it | 主语 | 代指物品 |
| made | 过去分词（make 的过去分词）| 被制造 |
| of | 介词 | 由……制成（保留在句尾）|

**注意**：`of` 不能省略——*What is it made?*（错误！）

### made of vs made from（详见 [[320-被动语态-was-made-of|被动语态-was-made-of]]）

| 介词 | 变化类型 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|
| made **of** | 物理变化（能看出原材料）| A chair is made **of** wood. |
| made **from** | 化学变化（看不出原材料）| Paper is made **from** wood. |

问句中通常用 `of`（更通用）：
- *What is it made **of**?*（标准问法）

### 时态变化

| 时态 | 问句 | 答句 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般现在时 | What **is** it **made of**? | It **is made of** silk. |
| 一般过去时 | What **was** it **made of**? | It **was made of** stone. |

### 扩展练习（Unit 5 主题）

| 物品 | 材料 | 句子 |
|------|------|------|
| T-shirt | cotton | A T-shirt is usually made of cotton. |
| shoes | leather / rubber | Shoes can be made of leather or rubber. |
| window | glass | Windows are made of glass. |
| ancient tools | stone | Ancient tools were made of stone. |

### 中考考点

1. `of` 不能省略（What is it made of? 不能变成 What is it made?）。
2. `made of`（物理）vs `made from`（化学）辨析。
3. 介词 `of` 提问，疑问词 `what` 代替宾语前置。
