## 它是在哪里发明的 / Where was it invented?

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**Where was it invented?** 就是"它是在哪里发明的？"——问某个发明的发明地点。

**问答练习**：

| 问 | 答 |
|----|-----|
| Where was the compass invented? | It was invented in China. |
| Where was the telephone invented? | It was invented in the United States. |
| Where was printing invented? | It was invented in China. |

**句型结构**：
- 问：**Where was** + it/物品 + **invented**?
- 答：It **was invented** in + 地点。

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型分析

`Where was it invented?` = 被动语态疑问句（一般过去时）

| 成分 | 词 | 说明 |
|------|---|------|
| Where | 疑问副词 | 询问地点 |
| was | be 动词（过去时，疑问提前）| 构成被动语态 |
| it | 主语 | 代指物品 |
| invented | 过去分词 | invent 的过去分词 |

### 被动语态疑问句结构

**一般疑问句**（Yes/No 型）：
- **Was** the telephone **invented** by Bell? → Yes, it was. / No, it wasn't.

**特殊疑问句**（wh- 型）：
- **Where** was it **invented**? → It was invented in China.
- **When** was it **invented**? → It was invented in 1876.
- **Who** was it **invented** by? → It was invented by Bell.

### 时态对比

| 时态 | 问句 | 答句 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般过去时 | Where **was** it **invented**? | It **was invented** in China. |
| 一般现在时 | Where **is** it **produced**? | It **is produced** in China. |
| 现在完成时 | Where **has** it **been** used? | It **has been** used in many countries. |

### 中国四大发明记忆表

| 发明 | 发明地 | 句子 |
|------|-------|------|
| 造纸术（paper-making）| 中国 | Paper-making was invented in China. |
| 印刷术（printing）| 中国 | Printing was invented in China. |
| 指南针（compass）| 中国 | The compass was invented in China. |
| 火药（gunpowder）| 中国 | Gunpowder was invented in China. |

### 中考考点

1. 被动语态疑问句语序：`Where/When/How + was/were + 主语 + 过去分词?`
2. 介词 `in` + 地点（invented in China，不用 at China）。
3. 与 `When was it invented?` 句型配对考查。
