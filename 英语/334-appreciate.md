## 🎓 appreciate（欣赏；感激）

### 词源与词族

**appreciate** 来自拉丁语 *ad-*（朝向）+ *pretiare*（定价），经由中世纪拉丁语 *appretiare*，本义"给某物定一个价值"，引申为"赏识、体认价值"。

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| appreciate | v. | 欣赏；感激；理解；增值（资产）|
| appreciation | n. | 欣赏；感激；增值 |
| appreciative | adj. | 表示感激/欣赏的 |
| appreciatively | adv. | 感激地；赞赏地 |
| appreciable | adj. | 可感知的；相当大的 |
| unappreciated | adj. | 不被欣赏/重视的 |

### 核心用法（三大含义）

**含义 1：欣赏（艺术、美、自然）**

| 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| appreciate music/art/nature | I appreciate classical music. |
| appreciate the beauty of | She appreciated the beauty of the landscape. |

**含义 2：感激（正式表达，比 thank 更正式）**

| 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| appreciate your help | I appreciate your help. |
| I would appreciate it if... | I would appreciate it if you could reply soon. |
| appreciate + doing | I appreciate being invited. |

> ⚠️ **关键结构**：`appreciate + doing`（不接 to do）  
> ✅ *I appreciate **being** invited.*  
> ❌ ~~I appreciate **to be** invited.~~

**含义 3：理解/认识到（严肃语境）**

- *I appreciate that this is a difficult situation.*（我理解这是困难的局面）
- *They failed to appreciate the risks involved.*（他们未能认识到其中的风险）

### 高考写作金句

- *I would appreciate it if you could...*（书信结尾万能句型）
- *I appreciate your taking the time to read my letter.*
- *We should learn to appreciate different cultures.*

### 中高考对比

| 场合 | 表达 | 正式程度 |
|------|------|---------|
| 感谢 | thank / thanks | ★★☆ 口语 |
| 感谢 | appreciate | ★★★ 正式 |
| 感谢 | be grateful for | ★★★ 书面 |

## 例句

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 我欣赏你的诚实。 | I appreciate your honesty. |
| 如果您能尽快回复，我将不胜感激。 | I would appreciate it if you could reply as soon as possible. |
| 她很欣赏那片壮观的景色。 | She appreciated the spectacular landscape. |
| 我们应该学会欣赏不同的文化。 | We should learn to appreciate different cultures. |
| 感谢您抽时间阅读我的信。 | I appreciate your taking the time to read my letter. |
