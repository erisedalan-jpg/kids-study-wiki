## 🎓 cycle（骑自行车；循环）

### 词源与词族

**cycle** 来自希腊语 *kyklos*（圆、轮），经拉丁语 *cyclus* 进入英语，本义"圆形运动"，引申为"循环"和"骑车"。

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| cycle | v. | 骑自行车；循环 |
| cycle | n. | 自行车（英式）；周期；循环 |
| cycling | n./adj. | 骑车运动；骑车的 |
| cyclist | n. | 骑自行车的人 |
| bicycle | n. | 自行车（通用说法）|
| recycle | v. | 回收利用 |
| recycling | n. | 回收利用 |
| lifecycle | n. | 生命周期 |

### 多义词用法

**含义 1：骑自行车（英式英语常用动词）**

| 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| cycle to school/work | She cycles to school every day. |
| go cycling | They went cycling in the countryside. |
| cycle along / around | We cycled along the river bank. |

> 注：美式英语通常说 **ride a bike**，英式英语则用 **cycle** 作动词更地道。

**含义 2：循环；周期（名词）**

| 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| the water cycle | The water cycle is essential to life on Earth. |
| life cycle | The life cycle of a butterfly includes four stages. |
| economic cycle | Economists study the economic cycle. |
| sleep cycle | A healthy sleep cycle is important. |

### 旅行与环保结合

自行车旅行（cycling tour / bike trip）是 Unit 2 旅行单元的重要主题，也与可持续旅游（sustainable tourism）相关：
- *Cycling is an eco-friendly way to travel.*（骑行是环保的旅行方式）
- *More and more tourists prefer cycling tours to see the local scenery.*
