## 🎓 damage（损坏；损失）

### 词源与词族

**damage** 来自古法语 *damage*，源自拉丁语 *damnum*（损失、罚款），本义"财产的损失"，引申为任何形式的破坏。

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| damage | n. | 损坏；损失（不可数）|
| damage | v. | 损坏；伤害 |
| damages | n. | 赔偿金（法律用语，复数）|
| damaged | adj. | 受损的 |
| damaging | adj. | 有破坏性的 |
| undamaged | adj. | 未受损的 |

### damage / destroy / ruin 辨析

| 词语 | 程度 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| **damage** | 部分受损，可修复 | The car was damaged in the accident. |
| **ruin** | 严重破坏，难以恢复 | The flood ruined the harvest. |
| **destroy** | 彻底摧毁，无法修复 | The earthquake destroyed the building. |

> 程度：damage（受损）< ruin（毁坏）< destroy（摧毁）

### 核心搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| cause damage (to) | 造成损坏 | The storm caused serious damage to the crops. |
| suffer damage | 遭受损失 | Many buildings suffered damage. |
| serious / severe / extensive damage | 严重损失 | The earthquake caused extensive damage. |
| environmental damage | 环境破坏 | We should reduce environmental damage. |
| brain/eye damage | 脑/眼损伤 | Staring at screens can cause eye damage. |
