## 🎓 fitness（健身；适合性）

### 词源与词族

**fitness** 由 **fit**（适合的；健康的）+ **-ness**（名词后缀）构成。**fit** 来自古英语，本义"合适"，达尔文进化论中的 *survival of the fittest*（适者生存）使该词广为人知。

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| fit | adj. | 健康的；合适的 |
| fit | v. | 适合；安装 |
| fitness | n. | 健康状态；适合性 |
| unfit | adj. | 不健康的；不合适的 |
| physically fit | 短语 | 身体健康 |
| fitness centre/gym | n. | 健身中心 |

### 核心用法

**"健身/健康"含义**：

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| physical fitness | 身体健康 | Physical fitness is important for everyone. |
| fitness centre / gym | 健身中心 | She goes to the fitness centre three times a week. |
| keep fit / stay fit | 保持健康 | Regular exercise helps you keep fit. |
| fitness training | 健身训练 | Athletes do fitness training every day. |
| mental fitness | 心理健康 | Mental fitness is as important as physical fitness. |

**"适合性"含义**（较书面）：
- *His fitness for the job was never in doubt.*（他胜任这份工作是毋庸置疑的）
- *The fitness of the candidate was assessed carefully.*

### 健康生活方式词汇网络

| 分类 | 词汇 |
|------|------|
| 运动类型 | aerobics, yoga, jogging, swimming, cycling |
| 健身设施 | gym, fitness centre, track, swimming pool |
| 健康目标 | lose weight, build muscle, improve stamina |
| 健康概念 | wellness, well-being, lifestyle, balanced diet |

### 高考写作常用表达

- *Regular exercise and a balanced diet contribute to physical fitness.*
- *More and more people are paying attention to health and fitness.*
- *A fitness routine can greatly improve your quality of life.*
