## 🎓 glance（瞥；扫视）

### 词源与词族

**glance** 可能来自古法语 *glacer*（滑行、掠过），15世纪进入英语，本义为"光线掠过"，引申为"目光扫过"。

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| glance | v. | 瞥一眼；扫视 |
| glance | n. | 一瞥；扫视 |
| at a glance | 短语 | 一眼看去；立刻 |
| glancing | adj. | 掠过的（glancing blow 擦过的一击） |

### "看"的词族辨析（高考高频）

| 词语 | 特征 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **glance (at)** | 短暂、随意地看一眼 | She glanced at her watch. |
| **stare (at)** | 长时间、目不转睛地盯着 | He stared at the screen for hours. |
| **gaze (at)** | 长时间、带感情地凝视 | They gazed at the stars. |
| **glimpse** | 短暂看见（不一定有意）| I caught a glimpse of the deer. |
| **glare (at)** | 愤怒地怒视 | She glared at him angrily. |
| **peer (at)** | 眯眼费力地看 | He peered at the small print. |
| **observe** | 仔细观察；注意到 | Scientists observe the changes. |

### 核心搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| glance at | 瞥一眼... | She glanced at the clock. |
| glance through / over | 快速浏览 | He glanced through the report. |
| at a glance | 一眼看去 | You can see the results at a glance. |
| catch a glance of | 瞥见 | I caught a glance of the actor. |
| exchange glances | 互相使眼色 | They exchanged glances. |
