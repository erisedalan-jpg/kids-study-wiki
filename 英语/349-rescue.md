## 🎓 rescue（救援；营救）

### 词源与词族

**rescue** 来自古法语 *rescourre*（重新夺回），源自拉丁语 *re-*（再次）+ *excutere*（摇出、驱除），本义"把某人从危险中解救出来"。

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| rescue | v. | 营救；解救 |
| rescue | n. | 救援；救援行动 |
| rescuer | n. | 救援人员 |
| rescue operation | n. | 救援行动 |
| rescue team | n. | 救援队 |
| search and rescue | n. | 搜寻与救援（SAR）|

### 核心搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| rescue sb from sth | 把某人从...中救出 | Firefighters rescued people from the burning building. |
| rescue operation | 救援行动 | A large-scale rescue operation was launched. |
| rescue team | 救援队 | The rescue team arrived within hours. |
| come to the rescue | 赶来救援 | Neighbours came to the rescue. |
| search and rescue | 搜寻救援 | Search and rescue teams searched for survivors. |
| rescue mission | 救援任务 | The soldiers carried out a rescue mission. |

### 救灾词汇群（Unit 4）

| 分类 | 词汇 |
|------|------|
| 人员 | rescuer, volunteer, firefighter, paramedic, soldier |
| 行动 | rescue, search, evacuate, assist, treat |
| 设备 | helicopter, ambulance, rescue boat, life jacket |
| 结果 | save, survive, recover, rebuild |
