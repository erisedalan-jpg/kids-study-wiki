## 🎓 定语从句（where / when / why）

> 前置：[[359-定语从句-高中入门|定语从句-高中入门]] 已讲关系代词 who/which/that  
> 本词条专讲**关系副词** where / when / why

### 三大关系副词总览

| 关系副词 | 先行词类型 | 在从句中作 | 可替换为 |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| **where** | 表地点的名词 | 地点状语 | in/at/on which |
| **when** | 表时间的名词 | 时间状语 | in/on/at which |
| **why** | reason（原因）| 原因状语 | for which |

### where 详解

**先行词**：place, city, country, school, room, moment（需表地点含义）

| 例句 | 可替换形式 |
|------|---------|
| This is the house **where** I grew up. | ...the house **in which** I grew up. |
| The city **where** she works is far away. | ...the city **in which** she works. |
| I remember the moment **where** everything changed. | ⚠️ 此处应用 when，因 moment 是时间词 |

### when 详解

**先行词**：time, day, year, moment, period, age（时间名词）

| 例句 | 可替换形式 |
|------|---------|
| I still remember the day **when** I first saw her. | ...the day **on which** I first saw her. |
| There was a time **when** people had no electricity. | ...a time **in which**... |
| The year **when** this happened was 2008. | ...the year **in which**... |

### why 详解

**先行词**：几乎只用于 reason

| 例句 | 可替换形式 | 注意 |
|------|---------|------|
| Tell me the reason **why** you are late. | ...the reason **for which**... | reason why 是固定搭配 |
| I don't understand the reason **why** she left. | — | why 可省略 |

> ⚠️ 注意：`the reason why` 后面的从句不能再用 because 引导  
> ❌ *The reason why he was absent was because he was sick.*  
> ✅ *The reason why he was absent was that he was sick.*

### 判断用 where 还是 which

关键：看关系词在从句中是否充当**成分**
- *This is the hotel **where** we stayed.*（stayed 不需要宾语，where 作状语）→ where ✅
- *This is the hotel **which** we booked.*（booked 需要宾语，which 作宾语）→ which ✅

## 例句

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 这是我长大的房子。 | This is the house where I grew up. |
| 我仍然记得第一次见到她的那天。 | I still remember the day when I first saw her. |
| 请告诉我你迟到的原因。 | Tell me the reason why you were late. |
| 从前有一段时间没有电。 | There was a time when people had no electricity. |
| 我长大的城市现在发展得很好。 | The city where I grew up has developed very well. |
| 我不理解她离开的原因。 | I don't understand the reason why she left. |
