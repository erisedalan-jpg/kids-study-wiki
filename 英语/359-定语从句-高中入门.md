## 🎓 定语从句（高中入门）

> 初中已学：who/which/that 基础（见 [[314-定语从句-初中入门|定语从句-初中入门]]）  
> 本词条：高中衔接 — 复习框架 + 引入 where/when/why + 限定 vs 非限定

### 知识体系总览

| 关系词类型 | 代词类 | 副词类 |
|---------|-------|-------|
| 指人 | who, whom, whose | — |
| 指物 | which, whose, that | — |
| 指地点 | — | where |
| 指时间 | — | when |
| 指原因 | — | why |

### 关系代词与关系副词的核心区别

| 类型 | 在从句中充当 | 例句 |
|------|-----------|------|
| 关系代词（who/which/that）| 主语、宾语、表语 | The book **that** I bought is interesting.（that 作宾语）|
| 关系副词（where/when/why）| 状语 | The city **where** I was born is beautiful.（where 作地点状语）|

> 判断方法：把从句单独拿出来，如果缺主语/宾语 → 用关系代词；如果不缺成分只缺状语 → 用关系副词

### 常见易错对比

❌ *The year when I born was 1990.*  
✅ *The year when I was born was 1990.*

❌ *The reason why he was late because he missed the bus.*  
✅ *The reason why he was late was that he missed the bus.*

❌ *This is the place where I visited last year.*（visited 不缺宾语但已有宾语→应用 which）  
✅ *This is the place which/that I visited last year.*

### 高考重点：only that 用法

- 先行词有 all/only/the very/the only/序数词/最高级 → 只能用 **that**，不能用 which
- *He is the only student **that** passed the exam.*
- *This is the best film **that** I have ever seen.*

## 例句

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 我出生的那座城市非常美丽。 | The city where I was born is very beautiful. |
| 我们相遇的那一年改变了一切。 | The year when we met changed everything. |
| 这就是他迟到的原因。 | This is the reason why he was late. |
| 我认识一个父亲是医生的人。 | I know a person whose father is a doctor. |
| 她是唯一通过考试的学生。 | She is the only student that passed the exam. |
| 这是我读过的最好的书。 | This is the best book that I have ever read. |
