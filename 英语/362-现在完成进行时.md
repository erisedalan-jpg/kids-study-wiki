## 🎓 现在完成进行时

### 基本构成

**have / has + been + 现在分词（V-ing）**

| 人称 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I/You/We/They | have been + V-ing | I **have been studying** for 3 hours. |
| He/She/It | has been + V-ing | She **has been running**. |
| 否定 | haven't/hasn't been + V-ing | He **hasn't been sleeping** well lately. |
| 疑问 | Have/Has + 主语 + been + V-ing? | **Have** you **been waiting** long? |

### 核心用法

**用法 ①：从过去某时开始，一直持续到现在（并可能继续）**
- *She **has been learning** English for 3 years.*（学了三年，还在继续）
- *They **have been building** the bridge since March.*（三月开始建，还在建）

**用法 ②：解释现在某一状态的原因**
- *Why are your eyes red? — I **have been crying**.*（刚才一直在哭，所以眼睛红）
- *She looks tired. She **has been working** all day.*

### 与现在完成时的区别（高考核心考点）

| 对比 | 现在完成时 | 现在完成进行时 |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 侧重 | **结果/状态**（动作完成了）| **过程/持续**（动作还在进行）|
| 例句 | I **have read** the book.（读完了）| I **have been reading** the book.（一直在读，未必完成）|
| 例句 | He **has written** three letters.（写完了三封）| He **has been writing** letters.（一直在写信这件事）|

### 不能用于完成进行时的动词

**状态动词**（非动作动词）不能用进行时：
- know, believe, love, hate, want, need, own, contain, seem, appear
- ❌ ~~She has been knowing him for years.~~
- ✅ *She has known him for years.*
