## 🎓 过去完成时

### 基本构成

**had + 过去分词（past participle）**

> 所有人称均用 **had**（不随人称变化）

| 句型 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定 | 主语 + had + pp | She **had left** before I arrived. |
| 否定 | 主语 + hadn't + pp | He **hadn't finished** his homework. |
| 疑问 | Had + 主语 + pp? | **Had** you **met** him before? |

### 核心概念：过去的过去

过去完成时表示**在某一过去时间点之前已经完成**的动作，是"过去的过去"。

**时间轴理解**：

```
更早的过去          过去某时          现在
    ↓                  ↓               ↓
had done --------→ 过去时间点 ----→ 现在
```

- *By the time I arrived, she **had already left**.*  
  （我到的时候，她已经走了——她走比我到更早）

### 常见触发词/结构

| 触发词/结构 | 例句 |
|-----------|------|
| by the time... | By the time he called, I had eaten. |
| before... | She had finished before he came. |
| after... | After he had left, I found his wallet. |
| when...（表"已经"）| When she arrived, the film had started. |
| it was the first/second time... | It was the first time I **had seen** snow. |
| hardly/scarcely...when | Hardly **had** I fallen asleep when the alarm rang. |
| no sooner...than | No sooner **had** she left than it began to rain. |

### 与一般过去时的对比

| 对比 | 过去完成时 | 一般过去时 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 时序关系 | 两件过去的事，强调哪个更早 | 只说一件过去的事 |
| 例句 | I **had studied** English before I went to the US. | I **studied** English in the US. |

### 叙事文写作应用（Unit 4 灾难叙事）

过去完成时在新闻报道/灾难叙事中非常重要：
- *When the rescue team arrived, many buildings **had already collapsed**.*
- *By the time help came, they **had been trapped** for 20 hours.*
