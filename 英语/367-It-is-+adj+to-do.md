## 🎓 It is + adj + to do（形式主语句型）

### 结构分析

**It is + 形容词 + to do sth / for sb to do sth / that 从句**

| 成分 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| It | 形式主语（空主语），代替后面的真实主语 |
| is | 系动词（可变时态）|
| adj | 描述后面动作或事实的形容词 |
| to do / that 从句 | 真正的主语 |

### 三种常见扩展形式

**形式 1：It is adj + to do**（最基本）
- *It is important **to study** English.*
- *It is difficult **to master** a language.*

**形式 2：It is adj + for sb + to do**（强调执行者）
- *It is important **for students** to read widely.*
- *It is impossible **for him** to finish in one hour.*

**形式 3：It is adj + of sb + to do**（描述人的品质，形容词表示对该人的评价）
- *It is kind **of you** to help me.*（你真好心帮助我）
- *It is clever **of her** to find this solution.*

> ⚠️ **for sb vs of sb**  
> - **for sb**：形容词描述事情本身（difficult, important, easy, necessary, possible）  
> - **of sb**：形容词描述人的性格（kind, clever, foolish, rude, brave, selfish）

### 常用形容词列表

| for sb to do | of sb to do |
|-------------|------------|
| easy, difficult, hard | kind, nice, good |
| important, necessary | clever, foolish, wise |
| possible, impossible | rude, polite, generous |
| dangerous, safe | brave, careless, selfish |

### 与主语从句的互换

- *It is important **that you study** hard.*（that 从句）
- = *It is important **for you to study** hard.*（不定式）
- = ***For you to study** hard is important.*（还原真正主语）

## 例句

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 努力学习英语是很重要的。 | It is important to study English hard. |
| 对学生来说广泛阅读是很重要的。 | It is important for students to read widely. |
| 你帮助我真是太好心了。 | It is kind of you to help me. |
| 在一小时内完成这项任务是不可能的。 | It is impossible for him to finish the task in one hour. |
| 在陌生的城市独自旅行是很危险的。 | It is dangerous to travel alone in an unfamiliar city. |
| 她能找到这个解决方案真是太聪明了。 | It is clever of her to find this solution. |
