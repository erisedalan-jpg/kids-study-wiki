## 🎓 as soon as（一...就...）

### 结构分析

**as soon as + 从句, 主句** / **主句 + as soon as + 从句**

- 表示主句动作在从句动作**之后立即**发生
- 从句是时间状语从句，引导词为 **as soon as**

### 时态规则（高考重点）

| 主句时态 | as soon as 从句时态 |
|---------|-----------------|
| 一般将来时 / 祈使句 | **一般现在时**（不用将来时！）|
| 一般过去时 | 一般过去时 |

> ⚠️ 关键规则：时间状语从句中**不用将来时**！  
> ❌ ~~Call me as soon as you **will arrive**.~~  
> ✅ *Call me as soon as you **arrive**.*

**过去用法**：
- *As soon as the alarm **rang**, she **got up**.*（闹钟一响，她就起床了）
- *He **left** as soon as the meeting **ended**.*

**将来用法**：
- *I **will call** you as soon as I **arrive**.*（我一到就给你打电话）
- *As soon as the rescue team **arrives**, they **will start** working.*

### as soon as 与相近词辨析

| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **as soon as** | 一...立即... | As soon as he saw me, he smiled. |
| **the moment / the instant** | 一...（更强调瞬间）| The moment I heard the news, I cried. |
| **immediately / directly** (BrE) | 一...（英式口语连词）| Immediately he saw me, he left. |
| **no sooner...than** | 一...就...（倒装，更正式）| No sooner had I sat down than he called. |
| **hardly/scarcely...when** | 刚...就...（倒装，更正式）| Hardly had I arrived when it rained. |

### 写作应用（Unit 4 灾难叙事）

- *As soon as the earthquake struck, people ran out of their homes.*
- *As soon as the rescue team arrived, they began searching for survivors.*
- *Call for help as soon as you find anyone injured.*
