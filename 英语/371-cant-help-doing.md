## 🎓 can't help doing（忍不住...）

### 结构分析

**can't help + doing（动名词）**

- 含义：忍不住做某事；情不自禁地做某事
- help 此处是实义动词，意为"避免（doing）"
- 否定句 can't + help + doing → "无法避免做" → "忍不住做"

| 形式 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| can't help doing | I can't help **laughing**. |
| couldn't help doing | She couldn't help **crying** when she heard the news. |
| can't help but do | I can't help but **think** about it.（后接动词原形）|

### 两种结构的区别

| 结构 | 语法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **can't help doing** | help = 避免；doing 是宾语 | I can't help **laughing**. |
| **can't help but do** | but = 除了；do 是动词原形 | I can't help but **laugh**. |
| **can't help it** | it 指代整件事 | I can't help it — it's just funny. |

> 两者意思相同，can't help doing 更常用于英式英语；can't help but do 在美式英语中也很常见。

### 与其他 "动名词" 句型对比

以下动词/短语后必须接 **doing**（不接 to do）：
- can't help **doing**（忍不住）
- enjoy **doing**（喜欢）
- finish **doing**（完成）
- avoid **doing**（避免）
- suggest **doing**（建议）
- mind **doing**（介意）
- give up **doing**（放弃）

### 常见情景与写作应用

- 表达自然情感反应：*She couldn't help smiling when she saw him.*
- 表达无奈：*I can't help worrying about the exam.*
- 高考写作：表达对美景/文化的情不自禁欣赏：  
  *Faced with such a breathtaking landscape, I couldn't help admiring the beauty of nature.*
