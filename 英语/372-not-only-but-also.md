## 🎓 not only...but also...（不仅...而且...）

### 结构分析

**not only A but also B**（不仅 A，而且 B）

- A 和 B 必须是**对等的语法成分**（名词对名词，动词对动词，从句对从句）
- **also** 可省略：*not only...but...*

### 三种使用场景

**场景 1：连接两个名词/形容词/副词**
- *He is not only smart **but also** hardworking.*（他不仅聪明，而且勤奋）
- *She speaks not only English **but also** French.*（她不仅说英语，还说法语）

**场景 2：连接两个谓语**
- *He not only **sings** but also **dances**.*（他不仅会唱歌，还会跳舞）
- *The earthquake not only **destroyed** buildings but also **killed** many people.*

**场景 3：连接两个句子（倒装！高考核心）**

当 **not only** 位于句首时，该分句需要**部分倒装**：

| 原句 | 倒装后 |
|------|-------|
| He is not only smart but also hardworking. | **Not only is he** smart, but he is also hardworking. |
| She can not only swim but also dive. | **Not only can she** swim, but she can also dive. |

> ⚠️ 规则：not only 置句首 → 该句助动词/系动词前移（倒装）；but also 后的句子**不倒装**

### 近义表达升级对比

| 表达 | 正式度 | 例句 |
|------|-------|------|
| not only...but also | ★★★ 正式 | Not only is she talented, but she is also dedicated. |
| both...and | ★★☆ 中性 | She is both talented and dedicated. |
| as well as | ★★☆ 中性 | She is talented as well as dedicated. |
| in addition to | ★★★ 正式 | In addition to being talented, she is dedicated. |
