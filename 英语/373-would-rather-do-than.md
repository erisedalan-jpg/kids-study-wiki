## 🎓 would rather do than...（宁愿...而不...）

### 结构分析

**主语 + would rather + 动词原形 + than + 动词原形**

- 两个 do 都是**动词原形**（than 后不加 to）
- would 常缩写为 'd

| 完整形式 | 缩写形式 |
|---------|---------|
| I would rather stay | I'd rather stay |
| She would rather read | She'd rather read |

### 四种用法详解

**用法 1：主语自己的偏好（最基本）**
- *I would rather **walk** than **take** a taxi.*（我宁愿走路也不坐出租车）
- *She'd rather **stay** at home than **go** to the party.*

**用法 2：would rather + do（不比较两件事，只表偏好）**
- *I'd rather **not go**.* （我宁愿不去）
- *I'd rather **stay** here.*（我宁愿留在这里）

**用法 3：would rather + sb + did（虚拟语气，表对别人的偏好——高中重点）**

结构：**would rather + 主语 + 动词过去式**（虚拟语气）

- *I'd rather **you stayed** at home.*（我宁愿你待在家里——现在/将来）
- *I'd rather **he didn't come**.*（我宁愿他别来）
- *I'd rather **you had told** me earlier.*（我宁愿你早点告诉我——过去虚拟）

**用法 4：would sooner（同义替换）**
- *I'd sooner walk than take a taxi.*（更口语，意思相同）

### 对比辨析

| 表达 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| would rather A than B | 宁愿 A 而不 B | I'd rather walk than drive. |
| prefer A to B | 喜欢 A 胜过 B | I prefer walking to driving. |
| prefer to do rather than do | 宁愿做 A 而非 B | I prefer to walk rather than drive. |
