# 灭绝的 / Extinct

> **一句话**：extinct 形容一个物种已经完全消失，没有任何存活个体；extinction 是其名词形式，指灭绝这一过程或状态。
> **English**: Extinct describes a species with no living members; extinction refers to the process or state of dying out completely.

---

## 🎓 词源·词族·搭配·高考考点

### 词源
来自拉丁语 *extinctus*（熄灭的）← *extinguere*（熄灭、消灭）。核心义：**彻底熄灭、不复存在**。

### 词族

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| extinct | adj. | 灭绝的；（火山）熄灭的 |
| extinction | n. | 灭绝；消亡 |
| extinguish | v. | 熄灭；扑灭（火） |
| extinguisher | n. | 灭火器 |

### 常见搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| become/go extinct | 走向灭绝 |
| extinct species | 已灭绝物种 |
| on the brink/verge of extinction | 濒临灭绝 |
| mass extinction | 大规模灭绝 |
| extinction event | 灭绝事件 |
| prevent extinction | 防止灭绝 |

### 高考考点
- extinct（已灭绝）vs endangered（濒危）vs threatened（受威胁）：程度递进
- 高考写作用法：If we don't act now, many species will go extinct.
- 注意：extinct 也可描述"死火山"（an extinct volcano），与灭绝义区分看语境

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 恐龙在约 6600 万年前灭绝。 | Dinosaurs went extinct about 66 million years ago. |
| 许多物种因人类活动而面临灭绝威胁。 | Many species face the threat of extinction due to human activities. |
| 渡渡鸟是最著名的已灭绝鸟类之一。 | The dodo is one of the most famous extinct birds. |
| 我们必须采取行动，防止更多物种走向灭绝。 | We must take action to prevent more species from going extinct. |
| 第六次大规模灭绝事件正在发生。 | The sixth mass extinction event is currently underway. |
| 濒危动物若不加保护，终将灭绝。 | Endangered animals will eventually become extinct without protection. |
