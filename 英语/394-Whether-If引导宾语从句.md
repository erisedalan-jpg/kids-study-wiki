# Whether / If 引导宾语从句

> **一句话**：whether 和 if 都可引导宾语从句表示"是否"，但在某些场合只能用 whether 不能用 if，需严格区分。
> **English**: Both "whether" and "if" can introduce object noun clauses meaning "whether or not," but in certain contexts only "whether" is correct.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 基本用法对比

| | whether | if |
|--|---------|-----|
| 引导宾语从句 | ✅ 可以 | ✅ 可以 |
| 引导主语从句 | ✅ 可以 | ❌ 不可以 |
| 引导表语从句 | ✅ 可以 | ❌ 不可以（少见）|
| 引导同位语从句 | ✅ 可以 | ❌ 不可以 |
| whether...or not | ✅ 可以 | ❌ 不可以 |
| 介词后 | ✅ 可以 | ❌ 不可以 |
| 与不定式连用 | ✅ whether to do | ❌ |

### 必须用 whether 的情况

| 情况 | 错误 | 正确 |
|------|------|------|
| 引导主语从句 | ❌ If he will come is unknown. | ✅ Whether he will come is unknown. |
| 引导表语从句 | ❌ The question is if we should go. | ✅ The question is whether we should go. |
| 与 or not 连用 | ❌ I don't know if or not he... | ✅ I don't know whether or not he... |
| 介词后 | ❌ It depends on if... | ✅ It depends on whether... |
| 与不定式连用 | ❌ I don't know if to go. | ✅ I don't know whether to go. |

### 宾语从句中 whether/if 可互换（但 whether 更正式）

- I don't know **whether/if** she will attend the meeting.
- He asked me **whether/if** I had finished my homework.

### 高考考点

| 题型 | 常见考法 |
|------|---------|
| 语法填空 | 主语从句位置填 whether（不能填 if） |
| 单句选择 | 介词后 / or not 连用选 whether |
| 书面表达 | 正确使用 whether to do（是否做某事） |

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 他是否会来是个问题。 | **Whether** he will come or not is a question. |
| 我不知道该不该去参加那个聚会。 | I don't know **whether** to go to the party. |
| 问题在于我们是否有足够的资金。 | The question is **whether** we have enough funds. |
| 这取决于你是否愿意付出努力。 | It depends on **whether** you are willing to make an effort. |
| 我想知道她是否喜欢这份礼物。 | I wonder **whether/if** she likes this gift. |
| 老师问我们是否完成了作业。 | The teacher asked **whether/if** we had finished our homework. |
