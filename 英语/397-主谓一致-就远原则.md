# 主谓一致：就远原则

> **一句话**：就远原则（也叫语法一致原则）指谓语动词与**主句核心主语**（离动词较远的主语）保持一致，适用于插入短语不改变主谓关系的结构。
> **English**: The "notional" or distance principle means the verb agrees with the main/head subject — even when a phrase is inserted between the subject and verb.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 适用就远原则的结构

以下连接词/短语**插在主语后**，不影响主谓一致，动词与**原主语**保持一致：

| 连接词/短语 | 含义 | 示例 |
|-----------|------|------|
| as well as | 也；还有 | Tom, as well as his friends, **is** going. |
| together with | 与……一起 | The president, together with his advisors, **was** present. |
| along with | 与……一道 | She, along with her classmates, **has** finished. |
| with | 带着 | The teacher, with all the students, **was** in the hall. |
| including | 包括 | Everyone, including the girls, **was** invited. |
| except | 除……之外 | Everyone except Tom **has** arrived. |
| besides | 除……之外（还有）| Nobody besides him **knows** the truth. |
| rather than | 而不是 | He, rather than I, **is** responsible. |
| in addition to | 除……之外 | The manager, in addition to his staff, **attends** daily meetings. |

### 与"就近原则"对比

| | 就近原则 | 就远原则 |
|--|---------|---------|
| 适用连词 | either...or / neither...nor / not only...but also | as well as / together with / with / including / besides |
| 动词与谁一致 | 离动词**最近**的名词 | 离动词**最远**（句子真正的主语）|
| 插入短语性质 | 连接词并列成分 | 附加说明短语（非并列）|

### 高考易错点

**错误**：
- ❌ Tom, as well as his friends, **are** going to the party.
- ✅ Tom, as well as his friends, **is** going to the party.（主语是 Tom，单数）

**"as well as" vs "and"**：
- Tom **and** his friends **are** going.（and 连接并列主语，用复数）
- Tom, **as well as** his friends, **is** going.（as well as 只是附加，主语仍是 Tom，单数）

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 校长连同全体教师出席了会议。 | The headmaster, together with all the teachers, **was** present at the meeting. |
| 她和同学们都已完成了任务。 | She, along with her classmates, **has** finished the task. |
| 每个人，包括女生，都被邀请了。 | Everyone, including the girls, **was** invited. |
| 除汤姆外，所有人都到了。 | Everyone except Tom **has** arrived. |
| 经理而非员工应对此负责。 | The manager, rather than his employees, **is** responsible for this. |
| 这项政策连同其修正案一起被通过了。 | The policy, together with its amendments, **was** passed. |
