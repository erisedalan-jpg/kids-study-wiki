# 关系副词 where / when / why

> **一句话**：关系副词 where、when、why 分别引导表示地点、时间、原因的定语从句，可与"介词 + which"互换，是高中定语从句的核心难点。
> **English**: The relative adverbs where, when, and why introduce relative clauses modifying nouns of place, time, and reason respectively; each can be replaced by a preposition + which.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 三个关系副词对照

| 关系副词 | 先行词类型 | 等价形式 | 示例 |
|---------|---------|---------|------|
| where | 地点名词 | in/at/on which | This is the city where I was born. = ...in which I was born. |
| when | 时间名词 | in/at/on which | I remember the day when we first met. = ...on which we first met. |
| why | reason | for which | That's the reason why he left. = ...for which he left. |

### 判断步骤（高考语法填空）

1. 看**先行词**：地点→ where；时间 → when；reason → why
2. 看**从句结构**：从句中缺少**状语**（非主/宾语），才用关系副词
3. 如缺主语/宾语 → 用**关系代词**（which/that/who）

### 关系副词 vs 关系代词辨析（高频考点）

| | 从句缺少成分 | 用词 | 示例 |
|--|------------|------|------|
| 关系代词 | 主语/宾语 | which/that/who | The house **which** I bought... （which 作 bought 的宾语） |
| 关系副词 | 状语 | where/when/why | The house **where** I live... （where 作 live 的状语） |

### where 的特殊用法

where 可修饰抽象"地点"含义的名词：
- a situation where（在……的情况中）
- a stage where（在……阶段）
- a point where（在……程度时）

例：We've reached a point **where** we must make a decision.

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 这就是我出生的城市。 | This is the city where I was born. |
| 我记得我们第一次见面的那天。 | I remember the day when we first met. |
| 这就是他辞职的原因。 | That's the reason why he resigned. |
| 春天是鲜花盛开的季节。 | Spring is the season when flowers bloom. |
| 博物馆是人们了解历史的地方。 | A museum is a place where people can learn about history. |
| 我不明白他为何如此固执的理由。 | I don't understand the reason why he is so stubborn. |
