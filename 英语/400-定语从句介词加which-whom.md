# 定语从句：介词 + which / whom

> **一句话**：当定语从句中的动词或形容词需要带介词时，可将介词提前至关系词之前，形成"介词 + which/whom"结构，是高中定语从句的重要进阶形式。
> **English**: When a verb in a relative clause requires a preposition, the preposition can be moved to the front, forming "preposition + which/whom" — a more formal structure.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 构成方式

以下三种形式等价，正式程度递增：

| 形式 | 例句 | 正式程度 |
|------|------|---------|
| 关系词在后（口语） | The house **that** I live in is beautiful. | 口语/非正式 |
| 介词后置（常见） | The house **which** I live in is beautiful. | 标准书面 |
| 介词前置（正式） | The house **in which** I live is beautiful. | 正式书面/高考偏好 |

### 常见介词 + which 结构

| 动词/形容词搭配 | 介词提前 | 示例 |
|--------------|---------|------|
| listen to | to which | This is the music **to which** she was listening. |
| apply for | for which | The job **for which** she applied was demanding. |
| be interested in | in which | The topic **in which** she is interested... |
| be fond of | of which | The painting **of which** he was fond... |
| depend on | on which | The method **on which** we depend... |
| contribute to | to which | The factor **to which** he contributed... |

### 介词 + whom（先行词为人时）

| 搭配 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| talk to → to whom | The man **to whom** she was talking is a professor. |
| work with → with whom | The colleague **with whom** I work is very kind. |
| look up to → to whom | The teacher **to whom** students look up... |
| refer to → to whom | The person **to whom** I referred... |

### 特别注意
- 介词提前后，**只能用 which/whom，不能用 that**
- ❌ in that I live（错误）
- ✅ in which I live（正确）

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 她申请的那份工作要求很高。 | The job for which she applied was very demanding. |
| 他和我一起工作的同事非常友好。 | The colleague with whom he works is very friendly. |
| 这是她倾听的那首音乐。 | This is the music to which she was listening. |
| 他们讨论的话题引起了广泛关注。 | The issue about which they debated attracted wide attention. |
| 我尊敬的那位老师退休了。 | The teacher to whom I looked up has retired. |
| 这是世界遗产申报所依据的标准。 | This is the standard on which the World Heritage nomination is based. |
