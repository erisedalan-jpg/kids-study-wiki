# 定语从句先行词为代词

> **一句话**：当定语从句的先行词是不定代词（all、everything、nothing、something 等）或指人的代词时，关系词的选择有特定规则。
> **English**: When the antecedent of a relative clause is an indefinite pronoun or a personal pronoun, there are specific rules for choosing the relative pronoun.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 先行词为不定代词时

| 先行词 | 用 that（不用 which） | 示例 |
|-------|---------------------|------|
| all | ✅ | All **that** glitters is not gold. |
| everything | ✅ | Everything **that** he said was true. |
| nothing | ✅ | There is nothing **that** I can do. |
| something | ✅ | Is there something **that** you want to tell me? |
| anything | ✅ | Do you have anything **that** can help? |
| little (抽象) | ✅ | There is little **that** we can do. |
| much | ✅ | Much **that** was said was misleading. |

**规律**：不定代词作先行词 → 只能用 **that**，不用 which

### 先行词为指人代词时

| 先行词 | 关系词 | 示例 |
|-------|-------|------|
| those（那些人） | who / that | Those **who** work hard will succeed. |
| one（一个人） | who / that | He is one **that** I can trust. |
| he / she / they（口语非正式） | who / that | She who hesitates is lost. |
| anybody / everyone / someone | who / that | Anyone **who** breaks the rules will be punished. |

### 先行词为"人+物"混合时

先行词同时包含人和物 → 只能用 **that**（不用 who 或 which）

例：She talked about the people and places **that** she had visited.

### 高考考点

| 题型 | 常见考法 |
|------|---------|
| 语法填空 | 填 that / who / which，先行词为代词 |
| 单句改错 | 将 which 改为 that（先行词为 everything 等）|
| 阅读理解 | 理解含不定代词先行词的定语从句语义 |

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 他所说的一切都让人深受启发。 | Everything **that** he said was inspiring. |
| 努力工作的人终将取得成功。 | Those **who** work hard will eventually succeed. |
| 我没有任何可以帮到你的东西。 | There is nothing **that** I can do to help you. |
| 他谈到了他认识的人和去过的地方。 | He talked about the people and places **that** he had known and visited. |
| 任何违反规定的人都将受到处分。 | Anyone **who** violates the rules will be punished. |
| 她说的话中有很多是不实的。 | Much **that** she said was untrue. |
