# 间接引语（高中）

> **一句话**：间接引语是转述他人话语的方式，需要按规则调整时态、人称代词和时间/地点状语，是高中语法的综合难点。
> **English**: Reported speech (indirect speech) is used to convey what someone said without quoting their exact words, requiring changes in tense, pronouns, and adverbials.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 时态后移规则（主句为过去时时）

| 直接引语时态 | 间接引语时态 | 示例 |
|------------|-----------|------|
| 一般现在时 | 一般过去时 | "I like music." → He said he liked music. |
| 现在进行时 | 过去进行时 | "I am reading." → She said she was reading. |
| 一般过去时 | 过去完成时 | "I saw her." → He said he had seen her. |
| 现在完成时 | 过去完成时 | "I have done it." → She said she had done it. |
| 一般将来时（will） | 过去将来时（would） | "I will come." → He said he would come. |
| 情态动词 can/may | could/might | "I can help." → She said she could help. |

### 人称代词变化

| 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 第一人称 → 看引述者（与主句主语一致） | "I love you." (Tom) → Tom said he loved her. |
| 第二人称 → 看受话者（被引述对象） | "You should try." (to Mary) → He told Mary she should try. |
| 第三人称 → 通常不变 | "She is kind." → He said she was kind. |

### 时间/地点状语变化

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| this week | that week |
| here | there |
| ago | before |

### 祈使句变间接引语

| 类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定祈使 | tell/ask sb. to do | "Open the window!" → She asked him to open the window. |
| 否定祈使 | tell/ask sb. not to do | "Don't be late!" → He told us not to be late. |

### 高考考点

| 题型 | 考查方式 |
|------|---------|
| 语法填空 | 直接引语 → 间接引语时态转换 |
| 单句改写 | 完成直接/间接引语互换 |
| 完形填空 | 段落中识别间接引语的时态逻辑 |

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 她说她很喜欢中国文化。 | She said (that) she liked Chinese culture. |
| 他告诉我他第二天要去北京。 | He told me he would go to Beijing the next day. |
| 老师要求我们不要在课上玩手机。 | The teacher told us not to play with our phones in class. |
| 他问她是否能帮他完成这个任务。 | He asked her if she could help him complete the task. |
| 她说她已经完成了作业。 | She said she had already finished her homework. |
| 父亲说那是他这辈子最自豪的时刻。 | Father said that was the proudest moment of his life. |
