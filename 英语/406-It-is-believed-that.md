# It is believed that...

> **一句话**：It is believed that... 表示"人们相信……"，是在学术写作和正式表达中陈述普遍认知的重要句型。
> **English**: "It is believed that..." means "people believe that..." — a formal impersonal structure commonly used in academic writing and formal discourse.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 结构说明

| 结构 | 含义 | 使用场景 |
|------|------|---------|
| It is believed that + 句子 | 人们相信…… | 表达普遍认知、研究结论 |
| 主语 + is believed to do/be... | 被认为…… | 正式报告、科普文章 |

### 等价变换

| It is believed that 结构 | 主语 + is believed to 结构 |
|------------------------|--------------------------|
| It is believed that the earth is round. | The earth is believed to be round. |
| It is believed that he committed the crime. | He is believed to have committed the crime. |
| It is believed that the species will recover. | The species is believed to recover. |

### 语气强弱比较

| 句型 | 确定程度 |
|------|---------|
| It is known that... | 确定性最高（已知事实）|
| It is believed that... | 较高（广泛认知）|
| It is thought that... | 中等（推测/分析）|
| It is said that... | 较低（传言/据说）|
| It is rumored that... | 最低（谣言/八卦）|

### 高考考点
- 写作中区分 believe 和 think：believe 更强调坚定信念，think 更随意
- 被动式 is believed to have done（过去完成式）：强调过去的完成动作
- 此结构在完形填空中可考查：They _____ to have lived there for centuries.（are believed）

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 人们相信教育是改变命运最有效的途径。 | It is believed that education is the most effective way to change one's fate. |
| 这种植物被认为有助于提高免疫力。 | This plant is believed to help boost the immune system. |
| 人们相信气候变化是由人类活动引起的。 | It is believed that climate change is caused by human activities. |
| 这座建筑据信已有超过一千年的历史。 | This building is believed to be more than a thousand years old. |
| 人们相信这一物种已濒临灭绝的边缘。 | It is believed that this species is on the brink of extinction. |
| 这名科学家据信在该领域取得了突破性进展。 | The scientist is believed to have made a breakthrough in this field. |
