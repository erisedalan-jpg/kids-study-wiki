# It is reported that...

> **一句话**：It is reported that... 表示"据报道……"，是新闻报道和正式写作中引述信息的规范句型。
> **English**: "It is reported that..." means "news reports say that..." — a standard impersonal passive structure for citing media information formally.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 结构说明

| 结构 | 含义 | 常见语境 |
|------|------|---------|
| It is reported that + 句子 | 据报道…… | 新闻报道、正式文章引用 |
| 主语 + is reported to do/be... | 据报道……（某人/事）| 新闻标题、正式报告 |

### 等价变换

| It is reported that 结构 | 主语 + is reported to 结构 |
|------------------------|--------------------------|
| It is reported that the earthquake killed hundreds. | Hundreds are reported to have been killed in the earthquake. |
| It is reported that he will resign. | He is reported to be going to resign. |
| It is reported that the company earned millions. | The company is reported to have earned millions. |

### 与其他信息传递句型对比

| 句型 | 信息来源 | 使用场景 |
|------|---------|---------|
| It is reported that... | 媒体报道 | 新闻写作 |
| It is said that... | 民间传言 | 日常/文化话题 |
| It is believed that... | 普遍认知 | 学术/科普 |
| It is announced that... | 官方公告 | 政府/机构 |
| It is confirmed that... | 官方确认 | 事实核实 |

### 高考考点
- 新闻类阅读理解中频繁出现此结构，需准确理解语义
- 写作中用此句型引述数据/事实，可提升书面表达的正式性
- 语法填空可能考：It is reported that the number of endangered species _____ (increase) rapidly.（has been increasing）

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 据报道，此次洪灾造成了严重的财产损失。 | It is reported that the flood has caused severe property damage. |
| 据报道，该国经济今年将实现5%的增长。 | It is reported that the country's economy will grow by 5% this year. |
| 据报道，他已被任命为新任首席执行官。 | He is reported to have been appointed as the new CEO. |
| 据报道，这座城市的空气质量近年来大幅改善。 | It is reported that the air quality of this city has improved significantly in recent years. |
| 据报道，该公司将裁员数千人。 | It is reported that the company will lay off thousands of employees. |
| 据报道，这项新发现将彻底改变癌症治疗方式。 | It is reported that this new discovery will revolutionize cancer treatment. |
