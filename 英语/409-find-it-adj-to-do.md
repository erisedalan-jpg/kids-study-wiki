# find it + adj. + to do

> **一句话**：find it + 形容词 + to do 是"形式宾语 it"结构，it 代替真正宾语（不定式），避免头重脚轻。
> **English**: "Find it + adj. + to do" uses "it" as a formal object standing for the real object (the infinitive phrase) — a common way to avoid front-heavy sentences.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 结构说明

| 位置 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 主语 | 做发现的人 |
| find | 主要动词（感官/判断类动词）|
| it | 形式宾语（占位，没有实际含义）|
| 形容词 | 宾语补足语（描述真正宾语的状态）|
| to do / doing / that从句 | 真正的宾语 |

**基本公式**：主语 + find/make/think/consider/feel + it + adj./n. + to do / that...

### 可替换的主要动词

| 动词 | 中文 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| find | 发现 | I find it difficult to understand. |
| make | 使得 | Technology makes it easy to connect. |
| think | 认为 | She thinks it necessary to review. |
| consider | 认为 | They consider it important to vote. |
| feel | 觉得 | He feels it wrong to cheat. |

### 形式宾语的三种真正宾语形式

| 真正宾语 | 示例 |
|---------|------|
| to do（动词不定式） | I find it hard **to learn** a new language. |
| that 从句 | He made it clear **that** he would leave. |
| doing（动名词，较少） | She found it tiring **waiting** all day. |

### 高考考点
- 语法填空：He found it difficult _____ (concentrate) in the noisy room. → to concentrate
- 写作中使用此结构可提升句式多样性：It makes it easier for students to access information online.
- 注意：it 不可省略！❌ I find difficult to learn.（语法错误）

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 我发现学习英语语法既难又有趣。 | I find it both difficult and interesting to learn English grammar. |
| 互联网使我们更容易获取信息。 | The Internet makes it easier for us to access information. |
| 他认为保护野生动物是每个人的责任。 | He thinks it everyone's responsibility to protect wildlife. |
| 她觉得在嘈杂的环境中集中注意力很难。 | She finds it hard to concentrate in a noisy environment. |
| 数字技术使远程教育成为可能。 | Digital technology makes it possible to study remotely. |
| 父母认为培养孩子的阅读习惯很重要。 | Parents consider it important to develop children's reading habits. |
