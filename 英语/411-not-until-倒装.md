# not until 倒装句

> **一句话**：not until 放句首时，主句需要倒装（助动词/情态动词/be 提前），是高中英语强调"直到……才……"的重要句型，也是高考倒装句的高频考点。
> **English**: When "not until" is placed at the beginning of a sentence for emphasis, the main clause undergoes subject-auxiliary inversion — a common inverted structure in formal English.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 基本结构对比

| 正常语序 | not until 强调句 |
|---------|----------------|
| He didn't realize the truth **until** she told him. | **Not until** she told him **did** he realize the truth. |
| I didn't know her name **until** we met again. | **Not until** we met again **did** I know her name. |

### 倒装规则

**公式**：Not until + 时间状语（从句/短语）+ **助动词 + 主语 + 谓语**

| 原句时态 | 倒装助动词 | 例句 |
|---------|----------|------|
| 一般过去时 | did | Not until then **did** he understand. |
| 一般现在时 | do/does | Not until now **do** we realize the problem. |
| 完成时 | have/has | Not until recently **has** the situation improved. |
| be 动词 | be 提前 | Not until midnight **was** the work finished. |

### 与 It was not until...that 强调句对比

| 结构 | 特点 |
|------|------|
| Not until...倒装 | 句首 not until，主句倒装 |
| It was not until...that | 强调句，that 后主句**不倒装** |

例：
- Not until midnight **did** he fall asleep.（倒装）
- It was not until midnight **that** he fell asleep.（强调句，无倒装）

### 高考常见错误

❌ Not until she came back, he left.（未倒装）
✅ Not until she came back **did** he leave.（正确倒装）

❌ Not until he failed did he realized the importance.（realized 应为 realize）
✅ Not until he failed did he realize the importance.

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 直到他失败了，才意识到努力的重要性。 | Not until he failed did he realize the importance of hard work. |
| 直到昨天我们才得知这个消息。 | Not until yesterday did we learn the news. |
| 直到见到她本人，我才相信这是真的。 | Not until I saw her in person did I believe it was true. |
| 直到最近，这种疾病的治疗方法才被发现。 | Not until recently has the treatment for this disease been discovered. |
| 直到深夜，他才完成所有的工作。 | Not until late at night did he finish all his work. |
| 直到她解释清楚，他才理解她的意思。 | Not until she explained clearly did he understand what she meant. |
