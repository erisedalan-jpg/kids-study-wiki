## 🎓 主谓一致（集体名词）

**定义**：集体名词（collective noun）在句中既可表整体（单数），也可表成员（复数），谓语动词的数取决于语境。

---

### 常见集体名词列表

| 集体名词 | 单数（整体）| 复数（个体成员）|
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| team | The team **is** winning. | The team **are** arguing with each other. |
| class | The class **is** large. | The class **are** doing different things. |
| family | The family **is** a unit. | The family **are** all abroad. |
| group | The group **was** formed in 2020. | The group **were** divided on the issue. |
| audience | The audience **was** large. | The audience **were** clapping. |
| staff | The staff **is** well-trained. | The staff **are** leaving early. |
| committee | The committee **has** decided. | The committee **are** disagreeing. |

---

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文例句 | 中文翻译 |
|----------|----------|
| The team was awarded the championship. | 这支队伍被授予了冠军称号。（整体）|
| The team were arguing about the best strategy. | 队员们在争论最佳策略。（个体）|
| The class is one of the best in the school. | 这个班是学校最好的班之一。（整体）|
| The family were all at different places during the holiday. | 假期期间，家人们都在不同地方。（个体）|
| The committee has made its final decision. | 委员会已作出最终决定。（整体）|
| The audience were cheering and clapping their hands. | 观众们在欢呼和鼓掌。（个体）|

---

### 高考考点

1. **判断整体 vs 个体**：看语境是强调集体作为整体行动，还是成员各自的状态/行为
2. **美式 vs 英式英语**：
   - 美式：集体名词通常用单数（The team **is**）
   - 英式：集体名词常用复数（The team **are**）— 高考以美式为主，但两者均可接受
3. **代词一致**：集体名词用单数时，代词用 it/its；用复数时，代词用 they/their
4. **语法填空**：确认集体名词后面动词及代词的数

### 易错点

- 注意：**a number of + 复数名词**（大量…，谓语复数）vs **the number of + 复数名词**（…的数量，谓语单数）
  - ✓ A number of students **are** absent. / The number of students **is** increasing.
- **police/people/cattle**：这些名词本身表复数（无 -s 但用复数谓语）
  - ✓ The police **are** investigating.
