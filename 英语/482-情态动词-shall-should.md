## 🎓 情态动词 shall / should

**核心用法**：shall 主要用于第一人称询问/建议；should 表义务、建议、推测、虚拟等，用途更广泛。

---

### shall 核心用法

| 用法 | 结构 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| 征求意见（第一人称）| Shall I/we...? | Shall I open the window? |
| 正式建议 | Shall we...? | Shall we begin the meeting? |
| 规定/命令（书面正式）| shall do | All members shall attend. |
| will 的过去式（间接引语）| would（shall 的过去式）| He said he would come. |

### should 核心用法

| 用法 | 结构 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| 义务/建议 | should do | You should exercise regularly. |
| 遗憾/批评（对过去）| should have done | You should have told me. |
| 推断（逻辑必然）| should be | He left an hour ago; he should be there now. |
| 虚拟语气 | should do（if 从句）| If you should need help, call me. |
| 情感从句 | should do | It's a pity that he should fail. |

---

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文例句 | 中文翻译 |
|----------|----------|
| Shall I carry that for you? | 我来帮你拿那个好吗？ |
| You should pay attention to body language. | 你应该注意肢体语言。 |
| He should have studied harder for the exam. | 他本应该为考试更努力学习的。 |
| The package should arrive by tomorrow. | 包裹应该明天前到达。 |
| It is important that everyone should be treated equally. | 重要的是每个人都应该被平等对待。 |
| Should you need any assistance, please let us know. | 如果您需要任何帮助，请告知我们。（正式条件句）|

---

### 高考考点

1. **should have done**（高频考点）：本应做但没做，含遗憾/批评
2. **It is + adj./n. + that + should do**（虚拟语气中的 should）：
   - It is important/necessary/vital that sb. (should) do...
   - 注意：should 可省略，but 动词原形保留
3. **should 表逻辑推断**：The train should arrive at 6.（应该会到）— 区别于 must（强推测）
4. **条件句中的 should**：If you should see him...（万一你见到他…，正式）

### 易错点

- **should have done（批评）vs must have done（推测）**：He **should have** studied harder.（批评）vs He **must have** studied hard.（推测）
- **虚拟语气 should 可省略**：It is vital that he (should) **come** on time.（come 用原形）
- **shall 用于第一人称**：Shall I...? / Shall we...? 不可用于第二/三人称（Should 可以）
