## 🎓 The reason why...is that...

**定义**：固定句型，用于解释某事的原因，"why" 引导定语从句修饰 reason，"that" 引导表语从句。

**基本结构**：
```
The reason (why + 从句) is that + 从句
The reason for + 名词/动名词 is that...
```

---

### 句型变体对比

| 句型 | 例子 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| The reason why...is that... | The reason why he succeeded is that he worked hard. | 标准完整形式 |
| The reason...is that... | The reason he left is that he was tired. | why 可省略 |
| The reason for...is that... | The reason for his success is that he is dedicated. | for 接名词/动名词 |
| That is why... | That is why she received the award. | 倒过来说原因结果 |

---

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文例句 | 中文翻译 |
|----------|----------|
| The reason why she achieved so much is that she never gave up. | 她取得如此大成就的原因是她从未放弃。 |
| The reason why body language matters is that it conveys emotions. | 肢体语言重要的原因是它能传达情感。 |
| The reason for the poor harvest is that there was too little rain. | 收成不好的原因是雨水太少。 |
| The reason why pandas are endangered is that their habitat is shrinking. | 大熊猫濒危的原因是它们的栖息地在缩小。 |
| The reason why she dedicated her life to science is that she loved it. | 她将一生献给科学的原因是她热爱科学。 |
| That is why innovation is so crucial in today's world. | 这就是为什么创新在当今世界如此重要。 |

---

### 高考考点

1. **关键搭配固定**：
   - The reason why + 从句（why 引导定语从句）
   - is that + 从句（that 引导表语从句）
   - 注意：不能用 because 代替 that：❌ The reason is because...（高考常见错误）
2. **That is why vs That is because**：
   - That is **why**（结果在前，原因在后）
   - That is **because**（原因在后）— 其实两者逻辑有差异，高考要求区分
3. **写作**：议论文解释原因的标准句型，提升逻辑表达

### 易错点

- **最高频错误**：❌ The reason is **because**...（reason 与 because 重复了原因含义）
- 正确替换：✓ The reason is **that**...；✓ The reason is + 名词从句
- **why vs that 从句功能不同**：
  - why 引导**定语从句**修饰 reason（定语）
  - that 引导**表语从句**说明 reason 的内容（表语）
