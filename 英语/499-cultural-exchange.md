## 🎓 Cultural Exchange 文化交流

**定义**：Cultural exchange 指不同文化背景的人或群体之间相互分享、学习对方文化（语言、习俗、艺术、价值观等）的过程，是跨文化沟通与理解的核心概念。

### 词形与词族

| 形式 | 示例 |
|---|---|
| 名词短语 | cultural exchange；intercultural communication |
| 形容词 | cultural (adj.) / cross-cultural (adj.) |
| 动词 | exchange (v./n.)：exchange ideas / an exchange student |
| 相关词 | culture, diversity, tradition, customs |

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文 | 中文 |
|---|---|
| Cultural exchange helps people understand and respect each other. | 文化交流有助于人们相互理解和尊重。 |
| She participated in a cultural exchange programme in France. | 她参加了一个赴法国的文化交流项目。 |
| The Silk Road was an ancient route for cultural and commercial exchange. | 丝绸之路是古代文化与商业交流的通道。 |
| Cultural exchange bridges the gap between different nations. | 文化交流弥合了不同国家之间的鸿沟。 |
| Through cultural exchange, we can learn to appreciate diversity. | 通过文化交流，我们能学会欣赏多样性。 |

### 高考考点

1. **话题写作**：Unit 2 Bridging Cultures 核心话题；写作常用句型：Cultural exchange plays a vital role in promoting mutual understanding between countries.
2. **定语从句（Unit 2 语法）**：Cultural exchange, which promotes mutual understanding, is important for world peace.（非限制性定语从句）
3. **搭配**：participate in / take part in a cultural exchange；promote / foster cultural exchange

### 易错点

- culture（文化，不可数）vs. a culture（某种文化，可数）；cultural 是形容词，不说 "a cultural exchange" 时注意 cultural 修饰 exchange。
- exchange 作名词：an exchange student（交流生）；作动词：exchange ideas/gifts with sb.
