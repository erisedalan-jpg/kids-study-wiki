## 🎓 Injure 受伤 / 使受伤

**定义**：Injure 作动词指"使（人/动物）受伤/受损"；injury 作名词指"伤/伤害"；injured 作形容词指"受伤的"。

### 词形与词族

| 形式 | 示例 |
|---|---|
| 动词 | injure (v.)：injure one's back / injure sb. |
| 名词 | injury (n., 可数)：a serious injury / suffer an injury |
| 形容词 | injured (adj.)：the injured player；an injured hand |
| 名词 | the injured (n.) 伤者（集合名词，复数含义）|
| 相关词 | wound (n./v.)；hurt (v.)；harm (v./n.)；damage (v./n.) |

### 词汇辨析

| 词 | 侧重点 |
|---|---|
| injure | 常指意外造成的身体伤害（偏正式）|
| wound | 刀枪等武器造成的伤口 |
| hurt | 口语化，泛指造成疼痛/伤害 |
| harm | 泛指伤害（身体/情感/利益）|

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文 | 中文 |
|---|---|
| He injured his knee during the football match and had to leave the field. | 他在足球比赛中膝盖受伤，不得不离场。 |
| The injured were taken to the nearest hospital by ambulance. | 伤者被救护车送往最近的医院。 |
| Serious injuries can occur if you don't wear protective equipment. | 如果不穿戴防护装备，可能会发生严重受伤。 |
| She suffered a head injury in the car accident. | 她在车祸中头部受伤。 |
| Apply first aid immediately to prevent further injury. | 立即施行急救以防止进一步受伤。 |

### 高考考点

1. **词形变化**：injure（动词）→ injured（形容词）→ injury（名词），语法填空高频：the **injured** passengers；suffer a serious **injury**。
2. **"the injured"**：注意 "the + 形容词" 表一类人：the injured = injured people（伤者）。
3. **被动语态**：He **was injured** in the accident.（be + past participle）

### 易错点

- injury 的复数：injuries（不规则，注意 y → ies）。
- injure 是及物动词，不说 "injure on the knee"，而是 "injure **one's** knee" 或 "be injured in the knee"。
