## 🎓 having done 作状语

**定义**：完成分词（having + past participle）作状语，表示该分词动作**先于**主句谓语动作完成，逻辑主语与主句主语一致。

### 基本结构对比

| 结构 | 时间关系 | 示例 |
|---|---|---|
| doing（现在分词）| 与主句动作同时 | **Walking** along the river, she felt calm. |
| having done（完成分词）| 先于主句动作 | **Having visited** the landmark, they headed to the next stop. |
| done（过去分词）| 被动或完成（侧重结果）| **Exhausted** by the journey, she fell asleep. |

### having done 的语义功能

| 功能 | 示例 |
|---|---|
| 时间（先完成）| Having finished the recipe, she tasted the dish. |
| 原因（已做了…所以）| Having lived abroad, he understands cultural differences well. |
| 伴随（完成某事后）| Having bandaged the wound, the nurse checked the patient's pulse. |

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文 | 中文 |
|---|---|
| **Having completed** the long journey, the team shared their experiences. | 完成了漫长的旅程后，团队成员分享了各自的经历。（时间）|
| **Having studied** Chinese cuisine for years, she could identify every ingredient. | 研究中国美食多年后，她能辨认出每一种食材。（原因）|
| **Having applied** first aid, he waited calmly for the ambulance to arrive. | 施行急救后，他冷静地等待救护车到来。（时间/原因）|
| **Having explored** the vast landscape of northwest China, we felt deeply moved. | 探索了中国西北部广袤的风景之后，我们深深地被感动了。（时间）|
| **Having been** to many countries, she speaks several languages fluently. | 去过许多国家之后，她能流利地说几种语言。（原因）|

### 高考考点

1. **时间先后关系**：having done 强调从句动作在先——这是与普通 doing 分词的本质区别。
2. **being done（被动进行）vs. having been done（被动完成）**：
   - **Being guided** by the map, they found the landmark.（被指引时，与主句同时）
   - **Having been guided** by the tour guide, they set off on their own.（被带领过之后，先于主句）
3. **独立主格中的 having done**：The journey **having ended**, they reflected on what they had learned.（独立主格形式）

### 易错点

- having done 的逻辑主语必须与主句主语一致（否则变成独立主格，需加明确的逻辑主语）。
- × **Having finished** the work, **the meeting was held**.（逻辑主语不一致：finishing 的主语是人，meeting 的主语是 meeting）→ 应改写为：**Having finished** the work, **they held the meeting**.
