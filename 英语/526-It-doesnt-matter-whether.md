## 🎓 It doesn't matter whether... 句型

**定义**：It doesn't matter whether (or not)... 意为"……是否……无关紧要/不重要"，it 是形式主语，whether 引导主语从句。是表达"不受某条件影响"的常用句型。

### 句型变体

| 句型 | 含义 | 示例 |
|---|---|---|
| It doesn't matter whether + 从句 | ……与否无关紧要 | It doesn't matter whether you're rich or poor. |
| It matters little whether + 从句 | ……几乎无关紧要（正式）| It matters little whether he agrees. |
| It makes no difference whether + 从句 | ……与否没有区别 | It makes no difference whether we go now or later. |
| Whether...or not, it doesn't matter. | 无论……与否，都无关紧要 | Whether she comes or not, it doesn't matter. |

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文 | 中文 |
|---|---|
| **It doesn't matter whether** you prefer Chinese cuisine or Italian food—both are worth exploring. | 无论你更喜欢中国菜还是意大利菜，两者都值得探索。 |
| **It doesn't matter whether** the robot looks human or not; what matters is what it can do. | 机器人看起来是否像人无关紧要，重要的是它能做什么。 |
| **It doesn't matter whether** you know first aid or not right now—you can always learn. | 你现在是否懂急救无关紧要——你随时可以学习。 |
| **It makes no difference whether** we take the northern or southern route; the journey will be long. | 无论我们走北线还是南线都没什么区别，旅程都会很长。 |
| **It matters little whether** the bridge was built by local or foreign engineers—what counts is its impact. | 这座桥是本地还是外国工程师建造的不太重要，重要的是它的影响。 |

### 高考考点

1. **形式主语 + whether 从句**：It doesn't matter **whether** + S + V...（whether 引导主语从句，it 是形式主语）。
2. **whether vs. if**：此结构只能用 whether，不可用 if（形式主语从句中 whether 专用）。
3. **与 What matters is 对比搭配**：It doesn't matter **whether**...; what matters **is**...（高考写作经典组合句式）。

### 易错点

- × It doesn't matter **if** it's raining.（条件状语从句可以用 if，但形式主语从句中表"是否"只能用 whether）
- × It doesn't matter **that** he fails.（that 引导表确定事实，whether 表"是否"，含义不同）
- matter 三单：it **matters**；否定：it **doesn't** matter（不是 "don't matter"）。
