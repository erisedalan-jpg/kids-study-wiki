## 🎓 On no account... 否定倒装句型

**定义**：将含有否定意义的副词或介词短语（如 on no account, never, seldom, rarely, not until, hardly...when 等）置于句首时，主句需要进行"部分倒装"（助动词/情态动词/be 提前），以增强语气或用于正式书面语。

### On no account 的用法

**On no account** = under no circumstances = 绝对不，无论如何不（用于强调禁止）

**正常语序**：You should on no account leave an injured person alone.
**倒装语序**：**On no account should** you leave an injured person alone.（助动词 should 提前）

### 常见否定副词/短语 + 倒装

| 否定成分 | 含义 | 倒装示例 |
|---|---|---|
| On no account | 绝对不，无论如何不 | **On no account should** you panic in an emergency. |
| Never | 从不 | **Never have** I seen such a dramatic landscape. |
| Seldom/Rarely | 很少 | **Seldom does** she eat unhealthy food. |
| Hardly...when | 刚……就…… | **Hardly had** he arrived **when** the emergency occurred. |
| Not until | 直到……才 | **Not until then did** I realise the importance of first aid. |
| Under no circumstances | 在任何情况下都不 | **Under no circumstances are** you allowed to move the injured person. |

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文 | 中文 |
|---|---|
| **On no account should** you apply pressure to a head injury without professional guidance. | 在没有专业指导的情况下，绝对不能对头部伤口施压。 |
| **Never have** I witnessed a more breathtaking landscape than that of northwest China. | 我从未见过比中国西北更令人叹为观止的风景。 |
| **Seldom does** a recipe from another culture feel completely foreign to us. | 来自其他文化的食谱很少让我们感到完全陌生。 |
| **Not until** the architect revealed the design **did** the public appreciate its brilliance. | 直到建筑师揭晓设计，公众才欣赏到其精妙之处。 |
| **Hardly had** the emergency started **when** she sprang into action. | 紧急情况刚一发生，她就立刻采取了行动。 |

### 高考考点

1. **部分倒装结构**：否定成分提前 + 助动词/情态动词/be + 主语 + 动词原形/过去分词。
2. **Hardly...when / No sooner...than**：Hardly had + 主语 + done...when + 主句（过去完成时 + 一般过去时）。
3. **写作提升**：在议论文结论或高分段落使用倒装句：Never should we underestimate the importance of cultural exchange.

### 易错点

- × On no account you should...（倒装时助动词必须前置，不说 "you should"）→ ✅ On no account **should** you...
- Hardly...when 中，hardly 所在从句用过去完成时，when 从句用一般过去时（非 when 用 had done）。
