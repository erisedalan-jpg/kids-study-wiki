## 🎓 overcome / 克服；战胜；压倒

**定义**：overcome（动词，不规则）意为"成功地对付或处理（困难、情绪等）；战胜"，也可表示被强烈情绪所压倒（be overcome by）。

### 词形变化（不规则动词）

| 形式 | 形式名称 | 示例 |
|------|---------|------|
| overcome | 原形 | We must overcome difficulties. |
| overcame | 过去式 | She overcame her fear. |
| overcome | 过去分词 | He has overcome many challenges. |
| overcoming | 现在分词 | Overcoming adversity builds character. |

### 例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 她凭借顽强的毅力克服了重重困难。 | She overcame numerous difficulties through sheer perseverance. |
| 要克服恐惧，首先要正视它。 | To overcome fear, you must first face it directly. |
| 他被那个消息的喜悦彻底压倒了。 | He was overcome with joy at the news. |
| 如果你当时寻求帮助，就会更快地克服那些障碍。 | If you had sought help, you would have overcome those obstacles more quickly. |
| 只要有勇气，任何逆境都是可以克服的。 | With courage, any adversity can be overcome. |
| 运动员需要克服身体与心理的双重挑战。 | Athletes need to overcome both physical and mental challenges. |

### 高考考点

1. **被动用法**：be overcome by/with + 情绪（被……所压倒）：be overcome with grief/joy/emotion
2. **不规则变化**：overcome — overcame — overcome，务必记忆
3. **写作高频**：overcome + difficulties/challenges/obstacles/adversity（克服困难/挑战/障碍/逆境）

### 易错点

- ❌ He overcomed the problem. → ✅ He overcame the problem.（过去式不加 -ed）
- ❌ She was overcome from sadness. → ✅ She was overcome with sadness.（用 with/by，不用 from）
