## 🎓 倒装——so/such 置首句型

**定义**：将 so + 形容词/副词 或 such + 名词短语置于句首以表强调，触发主谓部分倒装，常与 that 结果从句连用，构成"如此……以至于……"的强调结构。

---

### 核心结构对比

| 正常语序（不强调） | 倒装强调形式 |
|-----------------|------------|
| It was so cold that... | So cold was it that... |
| She spoke so fast that... | So fast did she speak that... |
| Such was his determination that... | Such was his determination that... |
| He was such a genius that... | Such a genius was he that... |

---

### 例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 天气如此寒冷，以至于学校停课了。 | So cold was the weather that school was cancelled. |
| 她跑得如此之快，以至于没人能追上她。 | So fast did she run that no one could catch up with her. |
| 他的决心如此之强，令所有人叹服。 | Such was his determination that everyone admired him. |
| 问题如此复杂，以至于需要团队合作才能解决。 | So complex was the problem that teamwork was required to solve it. |
| 他的演讲令人如此印象深刻，以至于全场掌声雷动。 | So impressive was his speech that the audience burst into applause. |
| 那是如此出色的一场表演，让所有人热泪盈眶。 | Such was the quality of the performance that everyone was moved to tears. |

---

### so 与 such 的选择规则

| 后接内容 | 用 so 还是 such |
|---------|--------------|
| 形容词 / 副词 | **so** + adj./adv. |
| 名词短语（不含形容词） | **such** + n. |
| 形容词 + 名词 | **such + a/an** + adj. + n. 或 **so** + adj. + **a/an** + n. |

---

### 高考考点

1. **倒装后助动词时态**：与正常语序保持一致（so fast did she run 中 did 对应 ran）
2. **so...that vs such...that 转换**：高考改写题常考
3. **写作强调**：用 so/such 倒装代替平淡的 it was very...，体现高级语法

### 易错点

- ❌ So cold it was that... → ✅ So cold was it that...（倒装要将 be 动词/助动词提前）
- ❌ Such he was a kind man that... → ✅ Such a kind man was he that... / So kind a man was he that...
