## 🎓 倒装——否定词置首（Negative Inversion）

**定义**：将具有否定意义的副词或词组置于句首，触发主谓部分倒装（助动词/情态动词提前），用于强调和书面正式语体。是高考倒装考点中频率最高的类型。

---

### 一、触发倒装的否定词/词组完整表

| 否定词/词组 | 含义 | 倒装示例 |
|-----------|------|---------|
| never | 从不 | Never have I seen such beauty. |
| seldom / rarely | 很少 | Seldom do we get such an opportunity. |
| hardly / scarcely | 几乎不 | Hardly had he left when it rained. |
| little | 几乎不（程度弱） | Little did I know what would happen. |
| not until | 直到……才 | Not until midnight did he fall asleep. |
| no sooner...than | 一……就…… | No sooner had he arrived than she left. |
| hardly/scarcely...when | 刚……就…… | Hardly had she sat down when the phone rang. |
| not only...but also | 不但……而且 | Not only did he sing, but he also danced. |
| in no way / under no circumstances | 绝不 | In no way will we give up. |
| at no time | 从不；任何时候都不 | At no time should you give up. |

---

### 二、例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 我从未见过如此美丽的夕阳。 | Never have I seen such a beautiful sunset. |
| 他刚离开，就开始下雨了。 | No sooner had he left than it began to rain. |
| 我们几乎没有这样的机会。 | Seldom do we get such an opportunity. |
| 他几乎不知道将要发生什么。 | Little did he know what would happen. |
| 直到她看到结果，她才相信自己成功了。 | Not until she saw the result did she believe she had succeeded. |
| 他不仅会唱歌，还会跳舞。 | Not only does he sing, but he also dances. |

---

### 高考考点

1. **no sooner...than 和 hardly...when 的时态**：主句用过去完成时（had done），从句用一般过去时
2. **not only...but also 倒装**：only not only 分句倒装，but also 分句正常语序
3. **语法填空**：看到否定词置首立刻判断是否需要倒装，并确定助动词形式和时态

### 易错点

- ❌ No sooner he had arrived than she left. → ✅ No sooner had he arrived than she left.
- ❌ Not until midnight he fell asleep. → ✅ Not until midnight did he fall asleep.
