## 🎓 虚拟语气——含蓄条件（Implied Conditional）

**定义**：条件并非通过 if 从句明示，而是隐藏在介词短语、名词短语、上下文或其他表达形式中，主句仍使用虚拟语气谓语形式（would/could/might + do 或 have done）。

---

### 含蓄条件的常见载体

| 载体形式 | 示例（含义） |
|---------|------------|
| **否定词** without / but for | Without your help, I would have failed. |
| **介词短语** with/without/in ... | With more time, I could finish it. |
| **名词短语** a brave man, a true friend | A real expert would know this. |
| **上下文隐含** | He would have come, but he was ill. |
| **otherwise** | I studied hard; otherwise I would have failed. |
| **分词/不定式** | Given more time, she could do better. |

---

### 例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 没有你的帮助，我不可能成功。（without 含蓄条件） | Without your help, I would not have succeeded. |
| 再给我一小时，我就能完成这项任务。（with 含蓄条件） | With one more hour, I could complete the task. |
| 一个理性的人在这种情况下不会这样做。（名词短语含蓄条件） | A rational person would not do this in such a situation. |
| 我学习很努力；否则我就会不及格。（otherwise 含蓄条件） | I studied hard; otherwise I would have failed. |
| 如果有更多时间，她本可以做得更好。（given 分词含蓄条件） | Given more time, she could have done better. |
| 但他病了，否则他早就来了。（but 连接上下文隐含） | He would have come, but he was ill. |

---

### 高考考点

1. **without / but for = if it were not for / if it had not been for**
   - Without water, nothing could survive. = If there were no water, nothing could survive.
2. **otherwise = if not**：是高考改错和语法填空的高频转换点
3. **识别信号**：主句出现 would/could/might，但没有 if 从句 → 找含蓄条件

### 易错点

- ❌ Without your help, I would not succeed yesterday. → ✅ ...I would not have succeeded.（过去的事用 have done）
- otherwise 是副词，连接两个完整句，不是连词
