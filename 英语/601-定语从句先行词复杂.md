## 🎓 定语从句：先行词复杂情况

**定义**：当定语从句的先行词是特殊形式时，关系词的选择和从句的处理会有特殊规则。

### 情况一：先行词为代词（it, this, that, all, anything 等）

| 先行词类型 | 关系词 | 示例 |
|-----------|--------|------|
| all, everything, anything, something, nothing | that（不用 which） | Everything that glitters is not gold. |
| the only, the very, the last + 名词 | that（不用 which/who） | She's the only person that I trust. |
| it（形式先行词）| that | It is knowledge that makes a man wise. |

### 情况二：先行词为整句话（非限制性）

| 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| which 指代前面整个句子 | He passed the exam, which surprised everyone. |
| which 修饰前句的某个结论 | She worked hard, which helped her succeed. |

### 情况三：含有介词的先行词

| 先行词含介词 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| the reason why/for which | 原因先行词 | That is the reason why he left. |
| the way in which/that | 方式先行词 | I like the way (that/in which) she speaks. |
| the time when/at which | 时间先行词 | I'll never forget the time when we first met. |
| the place where/in which | 地点先行词 | This is the place where/in which I was born. |

### 例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English | 说明 |
|------|---------|------|
| 闪闪发光的不一定都是金子。 | Everything that glitters is not gold. | everything 后用 that |
| 他是唯一能解决这个问题的人。 | He is the only one that can solve this problem. | the only 后用 that |
| 她迟到了，这让大家都很惊讶。 | She was late, which surprised everyone. | which 指代整句 |
| 这就是他失败的原因。 | That is the reason why he failed. | reason 作先行词 |
| 我喜欢她说话的方式。 | I like the way (that) she speaks. | way 作先行词 |
| 这是我第一次看到大海的地方。 | This is the place where I first saw the sea. | place 作先行词 |

### 高考考点

1. **只用 that 不用 which/who 的情况**：
   - 先行词为：all, everything, nothing, anything, the only/very/first + n.
   - 主句为 There be... 结构：There is nothing that can stop us.
2. **which 指代整句**（非限制性）：逗号 + which，指代整个主句内容
3. **关系副词与介词+which 互换**：
   - where = in which（地点）
   - when = at/on/in which（时间）
   - why = for which（原因）

### 易错点

- 先行词是 everything/anything 时必须用 that，不用 which：
  - ❌ Everything which he said was true.
  - ✅ Everything that he said was true.
- which 指代整句时，前面必须有逗号（非限制性）：
  - ✅ He left early, which was unexpected.（正确）
  - ❌ He left early which was unexpected.（缺逗号）
