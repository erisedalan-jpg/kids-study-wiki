## 🎓 Considering the fact that 考虑到……这一事实

**结构**：Considering the fact that + 从句, + 主句
**语义**：将某已知事实纳入考量，进而得出结论或提出建议，带有主动"考量"的意味

**近义表达**：
- Given the fact that...（鉴于……事实，较客观）
- Taking into consideration that...（将……纳入考量）
- Bearing in mind that...（记住……的前提下）
- In view of the fact that...（鉴于……事实）
- Given that...（鉴于……）

### 例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 考虑到这一地区的历史文化价值，保护这些建筑至关重要。 | Considering the fact that this area has great historical and cultural value, preserving these buildings is of paramount importance. |
| 考虑到他的年龄和经验，这次表现已经相当出色了。 | Considering the fact that he is young and inexperienced, his performance was quite impressive. |
| 考虑到全球气候变化的紧迫性，各国应立即采取行动。 | Considering the fact that global climate change is urgent, all countries should take immediate action. |
| 考虑到学生的学习压力，适当的课外活动有助于减轻焦虑。 | Considering the fact that students face heavy academic pressure, appropriate extracurricular activities help reduce anxiety. |
| 考虑到这一政策对经济的长期影响，我们应审慎决策。 | Considering the fact that this policy will have long-term economic effects, we should make decisions cautiously. |
| 考虑到网络信息良莠不齐，培养批判性思维至关重要。 | Considering the fact that online information varies greatly in quality, developing critical thinking is essential. |

### 高考写作运用

**适用场景**：
- 议论文体主体段落提出论据
- 评价某现象时引入客观背景
- 说明建议或结论的理由

**高分示范段**：
> Considering the fact that screen time has increased dramatically among teenagers, it is imperative that parents and schools work together to establish healthy digital habits. Encouraging physical activity and face-to-face interaction remains equally important.
>
> 考虑到青少年的屏幕时间急剧增加这一事实，家长和学校携手建立健康的数字习惯至关重要。同时，鼓励体育锻炼和面对面交流同样重要。

### 句型简化版（更灵活运用）

| 完整版 | 简化版 | 含义 |
|-------|-------|------|
| Considering the fact that... | Considering that... | 考虑到…… |
| Given the fact that... | Given that... | 鉴于…… |
| Taking into account the fact that... | Taking into account that... | 将……纳入考量 |

### 易错点

- considering 在此是介词/现在分词，不是动词；后面的主句主语可以与 considering 的逻辑主语不同（分词不悬垂，因为 considering 是介词）
  - ✅ Considering the fact that it rained, the match was postponed.（分析：这里 considering 是独立结构，合法）
- 注意与 Given the fact that 的区别：considering 更强调"主动考量"，given 更强调"客观事实"
