## 🎓 Given the fact that 鉴于……这一事实；考虑到……

**结构**：Given the fact that + 从句, + 主句
**语义**：提出已知的事实或背景，用来支持后续的结论或论点，相当于"考虑到……这一事实，……"

**近义表达**：
- Considering the fact that...（考虑到……）
- In light of the fact that...（鉴于……的事实）
- Given that...（鉴于……）
- Taking into account the fact that...（考虑到……）
- In view of the fact that...（鉴于……）

### 例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 鉴于环境污染日益严重这一事实，政府必须采取更严格的措施。 | Given the fact that environmental pollution is worsening, the government must take stricter measures. |
| 考虑到人口老龄化这一现实，社会保障制度需要改革。 | Given the fact that the population is ageing, the social security system needs reform. |
| 鉴于科技发展的速度，年轻人需要不断更新技能。 | Given the fact that technology is advancing rapidly, young people need to continuously update their skills. |
| 考虑到这一地区的教育资源匮乏，建设更多学校迫在眉睫。 | Given the fact that educational resources in this region are scarce, building more schools is urgent. |
| 鉴于气候变化对全球的威胁，国际合作至关重要。 | Given the fact that climate change poses a global threat, international cooperation is essential. |
| 考虑到当前经济形势，消费者应更加谨慎地理财。 | Given the fact that the current economic situation is challenging, consumers should manage their finances more carefully. |

### 高考写作运用

**使用场景**：
- 议论文的开头段引入背景和论点
- 主体段落的论点支撑
- 说明某政策或行动合理性

**高分段落示范**：
> Given the fact that social media has become an indispensable part of modern life, it is crucial for young people to develop strong media literacy skills. Only by distinguishing reliable information from misinformation can they make informed decisions.
>
> 鉴于社交媒体已成为现代生活不可或缺的一部分，年轻人培养强大的媒体素养技能至关重要。只有辨别可靠信息与虚假信息，他们才能做出明智的决定。

### 与 Considering 的区别

| 表达 | 侧重点 |
|------|-------|
| Given the fact that | 强调"基于已知事实"，客观陈述 |
| Considering the fact that | 强调"将……纳入考量"，主观参与 |
| In light of | 强调"在……的背景下/启发下" |

### 易错点

- given the fact that 后接完整从句（主语+谓语）：
  - ✅ Given the fact that technology is advancing...
  - ❌ Given the fact that the advancement of technology...（缺谓语）
- given 此处是介词（不是动词），后接 the fact that 从句
