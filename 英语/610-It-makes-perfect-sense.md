## 🎓 It makes perfect sense 这完全合情合理；说得通

**结构**：
- It makes perfect sense (that + 从句)
- It makes perfect sense to do sth.
- It makes sense（语气稍弱，一般合理）

**语义**：表达某观点、决定或做法是完全合理的、合乎逻辑的，有"不言而喻"的意味

**近义表达**：
- It is reasonable that...（……是合理的）
- It makes sense that...（……说得通）
- It is logical that...（……是合乎逻辑的）
- It is only natural that...（……是理所当然的）

### 例句（中英对照）

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 在信息时代，媒体素养教育完全合情合理。 | It makes perfect sense to teach media literacy in the information age. |
| 既然科技如此发达，利用人工智能提高效率完全说得通。 | It makes perfect sense that AI is used to improve efficiency, given how advanced technology has become. |
| 政府投资于可再生能源完全合情合理。 | It makes perfect sense for governments to invest in renewable energy. |
| 在竞争激烈的社会中，终身学习说得通。 | It makes perfect sense to pursue lifelong learning in a highly competitive society. |
| 年轻人学习多种语言完全合理，因为这能开阔视野。 | It makes perfect sense for young people to learn multiple languages, as it broadens their horizons. |
| 这项政策完全合情合理，因为它兼顾了经济发展和环境保护。 | This policy makes perfect sense, as it balances economic development with environmental protection. |

### 高考写作运用

**适用场景**：
- 议论文中支持某一立场或建议时
- 说明某决策或政策合理性时
- 对比两个观点时，支持其中一方

**高分示范句**：
> It makes perfect sense that schools should prioritise mental health education alongside academic learning. After all, a healthy mind is the foundation of all achievement.
>
> 学校将心理健康教育与学术学习并重，这完全合情合理。毕竟，健康的心理是所有成就的基础。

### 句型变体

| 变体 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| It makes sense | 普通程度的合理 | It makes sense to save money. |
| It makes perfect sense | 强调完全合理 | It makes perfect sense to invest in education. |
| It doesn't make sense | 不合理 | It doesn't make sense to waste resources. |
| Make sense of | 理解/弄明白 | I can't make sense of this problem. |

### 易错点

- 注意 to do 结构：It makes perfect sense **to** invest（不是 that invest）
- that 从句版：It makes perfect sense **that** we invest（需要 that）
- 固定短语：make sense of（理解……）vs make sense（合理）——含义不同
