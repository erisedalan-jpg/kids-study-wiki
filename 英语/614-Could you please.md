# Could you please...?

> **一句话**：**Could you please + 动词原形 ...?** 是英语中最常用的礼貌请求句型，用 *could*（虚拟过去式）+ *please* 双重缓和语气，比 *Can you...?* 更有礼貌。
> **English**: *Could you please + base verb...?* is the most common polite request structure in English, using *could* (subjunctive past) and *please* together to soften the request — more polite than *Can you...?*

---

## 📚 6-12 岁

要请别人帮忙时，前面加 **Could you please** 比直接说命令好得多。

例：
- ❌ Open the door. （命令）
- ✅ Could you please open the door? （礼貌）

回答：
- 同意：Sure / OK / No problem / Of course
- 拒绝：Sorry, I can't / Sorry, but...

## 🎓 给 12+

**核心结构**：**Could you please + 动词原形(...) ?**
- 注意：动词必须用**原形**（不变化）
- 注意：please 可放句中（Could you please...?）或句末（Could you...，please?）

**例句**：
| 中文 | English |
|---|---|
| 请把那本书递给我好吗？ | Could you please pass me that book? |
| 请把电视声音调小好吗？ | Could you please turn down the TV? |
| 请等我一下好吗？ | Could you please wait for me? |
| 请帮我个忙好吗？ | Could you please do me a favor? |

**应答方式**：
- ✓ 肯定：*Sure / Of course / No problem / Certainly / Yes, I'd be happy to.*
- ✗ 否定：*Sorry, I can't / I'm afraid I can't / I'd love to, but...*

**礼貌程度比较**：
1. Open the door.（命令）
2. Please open the door.（礼貌但仍偏直接）
3. Can you open the door?（询问能力 + 请求）
4. Could you open the door?（更委婉）
5. **Could you please open the door?**（双重礼貌）
6. Would you mind opening the door?（最委婉，注意后接 v-ing）

**与其它请求句型区别**：
- **Could you...?** 询问对方有无能力做（实际是请求）
- **Would you...?** 询问对方有无意愿做
- **Would you mind doing...?** 婉转询问"是否介意"
- **Will you...?** 较直接（家人朋友间）
