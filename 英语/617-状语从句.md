# 状语从句

> **一句话**：状语从句是**作主句状语**的从句，由特定**从属连词**引导，可表达时间、原因、条件、结果、目的、让步、比较、方式、地点九种语义关系，是英语复合句最重要的从句类型。
> **English**: An *adverbial clause* functions as an adverbial modifier of the main clause, introduced by subordinating conjunctions; it can express nine semantic relations — time, reason, condition, result, purpose, concession, comparison, manner, and place.

---

## 📚 6-12 岁

英语句子中，**主句**是主要意思，**从句**是给主句加上"什么时候、为什么、如果怎样、要多……"等附加信息。

例：
- I will go to school **when it stops raining**.（雨停时，我去上学）— 时间从句
- He didn't come **because he was sick**.（他没来，因为他生病）— 原因从句

引导这些从句的词叫**从属连词**：when/if/because/although/so that…

## 🎓 给 12+

**九大类型**：
| 类型 | 引导词 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| **时间** | when, while, as, before, after, until, since, as soon as, by the time | I'll call you **when I arrive**. |
| **原因** | because, since, as, now that | **Since you're free**, let's go. |
| **条件** | if, unless, as long as, in case, provided that | **If it rains**, we won't go. |
| **结果** | so...that, such...that, so that | She is **so smart that** everyone likes her. |
| **目的** | so that, in order that | I work hard **so that I can pass**. |
| **让步** | although, though, even if, even though, no matter | **Although** it's late, I keep working. |
| **比较** | than, as...as, as if | He is taller **than I am**. |
| **方式** | as, as if, as though | Do **as I told you**. |
| **地点** | where, wherever | Stay **where you are**. |

**核心语法点**：
1. **时间/条件主将从现**：主句一般将来时，从句用一般现在时
   - When it **rains**, I will stay home.（不是 will rain）
2. **so...that vs such...that**：
   - so + 形/副 + that：so beautiful that...
   - such + 名词短语 + that：such a beautiful day that...
3. **though/although 不能与 but 同时使用**：
   - ❌ ~~Although he is poor, but he is happy.~~
   - ✅ Although he is poor, he is happy.
4. **as 一词多义**：可引导原因（as you know）、时间（as I was walking）、方式（as I said）、比较（as tall as）、让步（young as he is）
5. **省略**：When (you are) young, you must learn.

**位置与标点**：
- 从句在前：从句, 主句（用逗号）
- 主句在前：主句 从句（无逗号）
