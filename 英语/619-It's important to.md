# It's important to...

> **一句话**：**It's important to + 动词原形** 是英语中表达"做某事很重要"的常用句型，**it 为形式主语**，真正主语是后面的不定式短语；可替换 important 为其它评价性形容词。
> **English**: *It's important to + base verb* expresses the importance of doing something; *it* is a placeholder subject and the real subject is the to-infinitive phrase; *important* can be replaced with other evaluative adjectives.

---

## 📚 6-12 岁

要说"做什么很重要"，用：
- **It's important to + 动词原形**
- 例：It's important to drink water.（喝水很重要）

类似句型（换形容词）：
- It's necessary to...（…是必要的）
- It's helpful to...（…是有帮助的）
- It's interesting to...（…是有趣的）
- It's hard to...（…是困难的）
- It's easy to...（…是容易的）

## 🎓 给 12+

**核心结构**：**It + be + adj. + (for sb.) + to do sth.**
- It（形式主语，无实际意义）
- be 动词（is/was/will be 等）
- adj.（评价性形容词：important / necessary / hard / easy / fun / boring / dangerous / impossible）
- for sb.（可省，表"对某人来说"）
- to do sth.（真正主语）

**例句**：
| 中文 | English |
|---|---|
| 学英语很重要。 | It's important to learn English. |
| 早睡很必要。 | It's necessary to go to bed early. |
| 帮助别人很有意义。 | It's meaningful to help others. |
| 对孩子来说，玩耍很重要。 | It's important **for kids** to play. |
| 在网上找到信息很容易。 | It's easy to find information online. |
| 一天学完一本书是不可能的。 | It's impossible to finish a book in one day. |

**形容词分两类**：
1. **描述事物**（用 to + 动词）：important / necessary / easy / hard / interesting / dangerous
2. **描述人的品质**（用 of sb.）：kind / nice / clever / silly / brave / generous
   - 例：It's kind **of you** to help me.

**特殊变体**：
- **It seems / appears / proves / turns out important to...**：似乎/证明…重要
- **It is important that + 主语 + (should) + 动词原形**：虚拟语气
   - It is important that everyone (should) be on time.

**与"主语 + 动词"对比**：
- It's important to read books.（更正式、更书面）
- Reading books is important.（动名词作主语，平实）
- To read books is important.（不定式作主语，较少用）

**注意**：
- ❌ ~~It is important learning English.~~（必须用不定式）
- ❌ ~~Important to learn English.~~（不能省略 It is）
- ✅ It is important to learn English.
