# 复合句

> **一句话**：复合句（complex sentence）是由**一个主句（main clause）+ 一个或多个从句（subordinate clause）**构成的句子——三大从句类型：**名词性从句、定语从句、状语从句**，是英语语法学习的核心和难点。
> **English**: A *complex sentence* consists of a main clause and one or more subordinate clauses joined by subordinators; three major clause types are noun clauses, attributive (relative) clauses, and adverbial clauses — the core challenge of English grammar.

---

## 🎓 给 12+

**句子按结构分类**：
| 类型 | 构成 | 例 |
|---|---|---|
| **简单句** | 一个主谓 | I love English. |
| **并列句**（compound）| 两个主句用 and/but/or 等连接 | I love English, **and** I love math. |
| **复合句**（complex）| 主句 + 从句 | I love English **because it's interesting**. |
| **并列复合句** | 含从句的并列句 | I love English **because it's interesting**, and I love math too. |

## 三大从句类型

### 1. 名词性从句（Noun Clauses）
**作用**：在主句中担任**主语 / 宾语 / 表语 / 同位语**。

**引导词**：
- **that**（陈述）：That he is honest is true.
- **whether / if**（疑问）：I don't know whether he will come.
- **what / who / which / when / where / why / how**：I wonder what he wants.

**四种功能**：
| 功能 | 例 |
|---|---|
| 主语从句 | **What he said** is true. |
| 宾语从句 | I think **(that) you are right**. |
| 表语从句 | The fact is **that he failed**. |
| 同位语从句 | The news **that he is leaving** surprised me. |

### 2. 定语从句（Attributive / Relative Clauses）
**作用**：修饰名词或代词（先行词）。

**引导词**：
- 关系代词：who / whom / whose / which / that
- 关系副词：where / when / why

**例**：
- The book **that I bought yesterday** is interesting.（that 关系代词）
- The man **who is talking** is my teacher.
- This is the place **where I was born**.（where 关系副词）
- I remember the day **when we first met**.

**限制性 vs 非限制性**：
- 限制性：紧跟先行词，无逗号 — The boy **who is wearing red** is my brother.
- 非限制性：用逗号隔开，附加信息 — My brother, **who lives in Beijing**, is a doctor.

### 3. 状语从句（Adverbial Clauses）
**作用**：作主句状语，修饰动词或全句。

**九大类型**：
| 类型 | 引导词 | 例 |
|---|---|---|
| 时间 | when, while, as, before, after | When I arrived, he had left. |
| 原因 | because, since, as | I'm tired because I worked all day. |
| 条件 | if, unless | If it rains, I'll stay home. |
| 让步 | although, though, even if | Although it's hard, I'll try. |
| 目的 | so that, in order that | I left early so that I wouldn't be late. |
| 结果 | so...that, such...that | He is so smart that he passed easily. |
| 比较 | than, as...as | He is taller than I am. |
| 方式 | as, as if | Do as I say. |
| 地点 | where, wherever | Stay where you are. |

## 关键语法点

### 1. 主从一致与时态呼应
主句过去时，从句通常也用过去时（或更早）：
- He said (that) he **was** tired.（不是 is tired）
- He said (that) he **had** finished.（之前完成）

### 2. 时间/条件主将从现
主句用一般将来时，从句用一般现在时：
- I will call you when I **arrive**.（不是 will arrive）
- If it **rains**, we won't go.

### 3. 引导词省略
- 宾语从句中的 that 常省略：I think (that) you are right.
- 限制性定语从句中关系代词作宾语时可省：The book (that/which) I bought is good.

### 4. 介词位置
- 正式：The man **to whom** I spoke is my uncle.
- 口语：The man **whom** I spoke **to** is my uncle.
