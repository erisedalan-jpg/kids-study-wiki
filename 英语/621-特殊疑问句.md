# 特殊疑问句

> **一句话**：特殊疑问句是用 **what / who / when / where / why / how** 等**疑问词**引导，**对句子某一部分提问**的句子——结构通常为"**疑问词 + 助动词 + 主语 + 实义动词原形 ?**"，**不能用 yes/no 回答**。
> **English**: *Special questions* (or *wh-questions*) are introduced by interrogative words (what / who / when / where / why / how) and inquire about specific information; structure: "wh-word + auxiliary + subject + base verb?"; cannot be answered with yes/no.

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁

**5W1H 提问词**：
- **What**（什么）：What is your name? 你叫什么？
- **Who**（谁）：Who is your friend? 你朋友是谁？
- **When**（何时）：When do you get up? 你什么时候起床？
- **Where**（哪里）：Where do you live? 你住哪里？
- **Why**（为什么）：Why are you sad? 你为什么伤心？
- **How**（怎么样）：How are you? 你怎么样？

**结构口诀**：**疑问词 + 助动词 + 你 + 动词原形 ?**

例：
- What **do** you eat? 你吃什么？
- Where **does** he live? 他住哪里？
- When **did** they go? 他们什么时候去？

回答时**不能用 Yes/No**，要给出具体信息：
- Q: What do you like? A: I like apples.（不能说 Yes / No）

## 🎓 给 12+

**核心结构**：

### 一般疑问句结构（对动作/状态提问）：
**疑问词 + 助动词 + 主语 + 实义动词原形 + 其他成分 ?**

### 当对主语提问时：
**疑问词 + 谓语动词 + 其他成分 ?**（无需助动词倒装）
- Who broke the window?（不要 Who did break the window?）

## 七大类疑问词

### 1. What（什么）
- 问事物：What is this? — It's a book.
- 问职业：What does he do? — He is a teacher.
- 问颜色：What color is your bag?
- 问时间：What time is it?

### 2. Who / Whom（谁 / 谁——宾格）
- 问主语：Who is your teacher?
- 问宾语（whom 较正式）：Whom did you see? / Who did you see?

### 3. Whose（谁的）
- 问所有：Whose book is this? — It's mine.

### 4. Which（哪一个）
- 在限定范围内选择：Which color do you like, red or blue?

### 5. When（何时）
- 问时间：When did you arrive? — Yesterday.

### 6. Where（何地）
- 问地点：Where is the bank? — On the corner.

### 7. Why（为什么）
- 问原因：Why are you late? — Because of the traffic.

### 8. How（如何）
- 问方式：How do you go to school? — By bus.
- How + 形容词：How old / tall / many / much / often / long / far

## 助动词的选择

| 时态 | 助动词 | 例 |
|---|---|---|
| 一般现在时 | do/does | What **does** he like? |
| 一般过去时 | did | When **did** you come? |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are | What **are** you doing? |
| 现在完成时 | have/has | Where **has** he been? |
| 一般将来时 | will | What **will** you do? |
| be 动词 | 直接倒装 | Where **is** he? |
| 情态动词 | 直接倒装 | What **can** you see? |

---

## 常见错误

1. **多用助动词**
   - ❌ ~~What does he likes?~~（错：实义动词回原形）
   - ✅ What does he like?

2. **对主语提问时多用助动词**
   - ❌ ~~Who did break the window?~~
   - ✅ Who broke the window?

3. **疑问词后语序错**
   - ❌ ~~Where you went?~~
   - ✅ Where did you go?

4. **回答用 Yes/No**
   - ❌ Q: Where are you? A: Yes.
   - ✅ Q: Where are you? A: At home.
