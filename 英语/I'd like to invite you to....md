# I'd like to invite you to...

## 句型解析

这是**礼貌邀请**的标准表达，比 "Can you come?" 更正式、更有礼貌。

**I'd like to invite you to** + 活动/地点/动词原形

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 基本结构

- I'd like to invite you **to my party**. 我想邀请你来参加我的派对。
- I'd like to invite you **to dinner**. 我想邀请你共进晚餐。
- I'd like to invite you **to join us**. 我想邀请你加入我们。

**注意**：
- I'd = I would（缩写）
- I'd like to = I want to（礼貌版）

### 接受/拒绝邀请的回应

| 接受 | 拒绝 |
|------|------|
| I'd love to! | I'm sorry, I can't. |
| That sounds great! | I'm afraid I'm busy. |
| Sure, I'll be there! | I have a piano lesson. |

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### 句型层次（从正式到口语）

| 正式程度 | 句型 |
|----------|------|
| 最正式 | I would like to invite you to... |
| 较正式 | I'd like to invite you to... |
| 普通 | Would you like to come to...? |
| 口语 | Can you come to...? / Do you want to come? |

### 完整邀请信格式

1. **邀请**：I'd like to invite you to...
2. **时间**：It will be on + 日期/时间
3. **地点**：It will be at + 地点
4. **等待回复**：Please let me know if you can come.

### 搭配 + 活动

- invite you to **my birthday party**（生日派对）
- invite you to **a picnic**（野餐）
- invite you **to have dinner with us**（共进晚餐）
- invite you **to visit our school**（参观我们学校）

## 对话示例（中英对照）

| 角色 | 英文 | 中文 |
|------|------|------|
| A | I'd like to invite you to my birthday party. | 我想邀请你来参加我的生日派对。 |
| B | Oh, that's very kind! When is it? | 哦，太好了！什么时候？ |
| A | It's on Saturday, November 15th, at 6 p.m. | 11月15日周六晚上6点。 |
| B | I'd love to come! Thank you for inviting me. | 我很乐意来！谢谢你的邀请。 |
| A | Great! My address is... | 太棒了！我的地址是…… |
