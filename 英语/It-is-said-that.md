# It is said that...

> **一句话**：It is said that... 是客观陈述"据说……"的正式表达，用无人称 it 作形式主语，避免直接说明消息来源。
> **English**: "It is said that..." is a formal impersonal structure meaning "people say that..." — used to report information without specifying the source.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 结构说明

| 结构 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| It is said that + 句子 | 基本结构，it 是形式主语，that 从句是真正主语 |
| 主语 + is said to do/be... | 等价变换结构（将 that 从句主语提前）|

### 等价变换

| It is said that 结构 | 主语 + is said to 结构 |
|---------------------|----------------------|
| It is said that he is rich. | He is said to be rich. |
| It is said that she has left. | She is said to have left. |
| It is said that they will come. | They are said to come. |

### 同系列句型（信息传递类）

| 句型 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| It is said that... | 据说…… |
| It is reported that... | 据报道…… |
| It is believed that... | 人们相信…… |
| It is known that... | 众所周知…… |
| It is thought that... | 人们认为…… |
| It is suggested that... | 有人建议……（注意：虚拟语气！）|
| It is announced that... | 据宣布…… |

### 高考考点
- 写作中使用此句型可提升表达正式程度
- It is suggested that sb. (should) do...（虚拟语气，should 可省略）与其他结构区分
- 被动语态考点：将主动句"People say that..."改写为被动式"It is said that..."

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 据说这座古堡已有八百年历史了。 | It is said that this ancient castle is 800 years old. |
| 据说他是这个领域最权威的专家之一。 | He is said to be one of the most authoritative experts in this field. |
| 据说新政策将于明年正式实施。 | It is said that the new policy will be officially implemented next year. |
| 据说这幅画是一位无名画家所作。 | It is said that this painting was created by an unknown artist. |
| 据说她已经拒绝了这份工作邀请。 | She is said to have turned down the job offer. |
| 据说这种草药有神奇的医疗功效。 | It is said that this herb has remarkable medicinal properties. |
