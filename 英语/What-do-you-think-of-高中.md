## 🎓 What do you think of...?（高中）

> 初中已学：[[What-do-you-think-of]]（基础用法）  
> 本词条：高中深化 — 多种"表达观点"句式 + 学术写作应用

### 结构分析

**What do you think of + 名词/动名词/名词短语?**

- *What do you think of **this film**?*（你觉得这部电影怎么样？）
- *What do you think of **his idea**?*（你对他的想法怎么看？）
- *What do you think of **studying abroad**?*（你觉得出国留学怎么样？）

### 相近句式大比较

| 句型 | 语气 | 场合 |
|------|------|------|
| What do you think of...? | 询问总体印象/评价 | 口语、半正式 |
| What do you think about...? | 询问想法/意见（about 更强调思考过程）| 口语 |
| How do you find...? | 询问体验/感受（英式）| 口语 |
| What's your opinion of/on...? | 询问正式意见 | 书面、正式 |
| What are your views on...? | 询问多角度看法 | 书面、议论 |
| How do you feel about...? | 询问情感态度 | 口语 |

### 表达观点的回应套话

**表示赞同**：
- *I think it's great / excellent / impressive.*
- *In my opinion, this is a brilliant idea.*
- *I agree that...*

**表示反对**：
- *Personally, I think it has some problems.*
- *I'm not sure about this because...*
- *I disagree because...*

**中立/辩证**：
- *There are pros and cons...*
- *On one hand... on the other hand...*
- *It depends on the situation.*

### 高考写作：观点表达升级

| 初级表达 | 升级表达 |
|---------|---------|
| I think ... | In my view, ... / It is my belief that ... |
| I like ... | I appreciate ... / I am in favor of ... |
| I don't like ... | I am opposed to ... / I have reservations about ... |

## 例句

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 你对这部电影有什么看法？ | What do you think of this film? |
| 依我看，这是个绝妙的主意。 | In my opinion, this is a brilliant idea. |
| 你对出国留学有什么看法？ | What are your views on studying abroad? |
| 我认为这既有优点，也有缺点。 | I think there are both pros and cons to this. |
| 你觉得我们学校的新规定怎么样？ | What do you think of our school's new rules? |
| 我持不同意见，因为我认为... | I disagree because I think... |
