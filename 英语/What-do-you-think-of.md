## 你对……怎么看 / What do you think of...?

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**What do you think of...?** 意思是"你对……有什么看法/感觉？"——询问别人的意见或感受。

**常用回答**：
- I think it's great! 我觉得很棒！
- I think (that) mooncakes are delicious. 我认为月饼很好吃。
- I don't think it's a good idea. 我认为这不是个好主意。

**类似句型**：
- What do you **think of** mooncakes? = How do you **like** mooncakes?
- What do you **think about** the new movie?

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型结构

```
What do you think of/about + 名词（短语）?
```

| 版本 | 例句 | 区别 |
|------|------|------|
| think of | What do you think **of** the movie? | 更常用 |
| think about | What do you think **about** the plan? | 强调思考过程 |

### 相关征求意见句型

| 句型 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| What do you think of...? | 你对……怎么看？ | What do you think of this idea? |
| How do you like...? | 你觉得……怎么样？ | How do you like Beijing? |
| What's your opinion of/about...? | 你对……的意见是？| What's your opinion of this rule? |
| What do you feel about...? | 你对……感受如何？ | What do you feel about this change? |

### 回答句型（含宾语从句）

回答 *What do you think of...?* 时，常用 **I think (that)...**：

- *What do you think of mooncakes?*
  → *I think **(that)** they are delicious!*

- *What do you think of the plan?*
  → *I think **(that)** it's a good idea.*
  → *I don't think **(that)** it will work.*

注意：否定放在 **think** 前，不放在从句中：
- ✓ I **don't think** (that) it's good.
- ✗ I think (that) it **isn't** good.（语法正确但不自然）

### 中考考点

1. `think of` vs `think about` 用法相近，均可询问看法。
2. 否定前置：*I **don't** think (that) it's good.*（不是 *I think it's not good*）。
3. Unit 2 核心句型，常与节日文化话题结合。

| 中文 | English |
