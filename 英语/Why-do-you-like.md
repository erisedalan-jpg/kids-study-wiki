## 你为什么喜欢……？/ Why Do You Like...?

**核心句型**：询问原因，用 because 回答

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**Why do you like...?** 就是"你为什么喜欢……？"——问别人喜欢某样东西的原因。

**问答规律**：**Why...? → Because...**（因为）

| 问 | 答 |
|----|-----|
| Why do you like pandas? | Because they are cute.（因为它们很可爱。）|
| Why do you like lions? | Because they are strong.（因为它们很强壮。）|
| Why do you like dogs? | Because they are friendly.（因为它们很友好。）|
| Why do you like English? | Because it's interesting.（因为它很有趣。）|

**常用理由**：

| 理由 | 英文 |
|------|------|
| 可爱的 | cute |
| 有趣的 | interesting / fun |
| 强壮的 | strong / powerful |
| 聪明的 | smart / intelligent |
| 漂亮的 | beautiful |
| 特别的 | special / unique |

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 句型变化（换人称/话题）

| 句型 | 例子 |
|------|------|
| Why do **you** like...? | Why do you like koalas? |
| Why does **she** like...? | Why does she like tigers? |
| Why do **you** want to be...? | Why do you want to be a doctor? |
| Why does **he** go to school by bike? | Because his home is close. |

### 扩展：Why / Because 在不同语境

**喜好（Unit 5 动物话题）**：
- Why do you like pandas? — Because they are cute and gentle.

**职业（Unit 11 话题）**：
- Why do you want to be a reporter? — Because I like writing.

**交通（Unit 3 话题）**：
- Why do you walk to school? — Because my home is very close.

### 注意：Because 不独立成句

在英语书面语中，because 引导的句子不单独使用：
- ❌ Why do you like pandas? Because cute.
- ✓ Because **they are** cute.（主谓不省略）
- ✓ I like pandas **because** they are cute.（合并成一句）

## 例句（中英对照）

1. Why do you like pandas? Because they are cute and gentle.（你为什么喜欢大熊猫？因为它们可爱而温柔。）
2. Why does she like tigers? Because she thinks they are beautiful.（她为什么喜欢老虎？因为她觉得它们很漂亮。）
3. Why do you want to be a teacher? Because I love children.（你为什么想当老师？因为我喜爱孩子。）
4. Why do you take the bus to school? Because it's faster than walking.（你为什么坐公共汽车上学？因为比走路快。）
5. Why do you like dumplings? Because my grandmother makes them so well!（你为什么喜欢饺子？因为我奶奶包得太好吃了！）
