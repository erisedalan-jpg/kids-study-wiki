## 🎓 award（奖项；授予）

### 词源与词族

**award** 来自古北法语 *awarder*（仔细看、决定），由拉丁语 *ex-*（彻底）+ *wardare*（看守）构成，本义"经过仔细审查后做出决定"，引申为"裁定给予"。

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| award | n. | 奖项；奖金；裁定 |
| award | v. | 授予；裁定给予 |
| award ceremony | n. | 颁奖典礼 |
| award-winning | adj. | 获奖的 |
| awardee | n. | 获奖者 |

### award / prize / reward 辨析（高考重点）

| 词语 | 性质 | 典型语境 |
|------|------|---------|
| **award** | 正式认可成就的奖项 | film awards, sports awards, academic awards |
| **prize** | 竞赛/比赛中的奖品或奖金 | win first prize; Nobel Prize |
| **reward** | 因做好事或努力而获得的报酬/回报（也可非物质）| get a reward for hard work; inner reward |
| **medal** | 勋章；奖牌（实物）| gold/silver/bronze medal |
| **trophy** | 奖杯（实物）| win a trophy |

> 记忆技巧：award（主动颁发认可）→ prize（竞赛奖品）→ reward（努力的回报）

### 核心搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| win / receive an award | 获奖 | She won an award for best actress. |
| award sb sth | 授予某人某物 | The judge awarded him first place. |
| be awarded | 被授予 | He was awarded the gold medal. |
| award-winning | 获奖的 | an award-winning film |
| award ceremony | 颁奖典礼 | The award ceremony was held last night. |
