# 修复 / Restore

> **一句话**：restore 表示将某物恢复到原来的状态，常用于建筑修复、生态恢复和健康恢复等语境。
> **English**: To restore means to bring something back to its original condition — used for buildings, nature, health, or order.

---

## 🎓 词源·词族·搭配·高考考点

### 词源
来自拉丁语 *restaurare*（重建、恢复）← *re-*（重新）+ *staurare*（建立）。核心义：**重新建立原有状态**。

### 词族

| 词形 | 词性 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| restore | v. | 恢复；修复；归还 |
| restoration | n. | 恢复；修复；复辟 |
| restorative | adj. / n. | 有恢复作用的；补品 |
| restored | adj. | 已修复的 |

### 常见搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| restore a building | 修复建筑物 |
| restore order | 恢复秩序 |
| restore sb.'s health | 恢复某人的健康 |
| restore confidence | 重建信心 |
| ecological restoration | 生态修复 |
| restore to its former glory | 恢复昔日辉煌 |

### 高考考点
- restore 与 repair、renovate 辨析：
  - restore：强调回到**原来的样子**（文物、建筑、生态）
  - repair：强调修好**损坏的部分**（日常物品）
  - renovate：强调**翻新改造**（房屋装修）
- 高考写作可用：The ancient temple was restored to its original appearance.

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 这座古寺经过多年修复，终于恢复原貌。 | The ancient temple was restored to its original appearance after years of work. |
| 生态系统修复需要全社会的共同努力。 | Ecological restoration requires the joint effort of the whole society. |
| 医生帮助他恢复了健康。 | The doctor helped restore his health. |
| 政府拨款修复被损毁的历史遗址。 | The government allocated funds to restore the damaged historical site. |
| 修复这幅名画花了整整三年时间。 | Restoring the famous painting took a full three years. |
| 和平条约的签署恢复了两国关系。 | The signing of the peace treaty restored relations between the two countries. |
