## 应该被允许 / Should be allowed

---

## 📚 给 6-12 岁（自读版）

**Should be allowed to** 意思是"应该被允许做……"——表示某人有权利做某事，这是合理的。

**公式**：主语 + **should be allowed to** + 动词原形

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 青少年应该被允许选择自己的衣服。| Teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes. |
| 学生应该被允许在课堂上提问。| Students should be allowed to ask questions in class. |
| 孩子们不应该被允许看太多电视。| Children shouldn't be allowed to watch too much TV. |

---

## 🎓 给 12+（进阶版）

### 语法分析

`should be allowed to` = 情态动词（should）+ 被动语态（be + 过去分词）+ 不定式（to do）

| 成分 | 词 | 功能 |
|------|---|------|
| should | 情态动词 | 表示应该/建议 |
| be | 助动词（被动）| 构成被动语态 |
| allowed | 过去分词 | 被允许 |
| to + 动词原形 | 不定式 | 指具体行为 |

### 肯定与否定

| 形式 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定 | should be allowed to do | Students should be allowed to choose their subjects. |
| 否定 | shouldn't be allowed to do | Teenagers shouldn't be allowed to drive. |
| 疑问 | Should sb be allowed to do? | Should students be allowed to use phones? |

### 相关辩论句型（Unit 7）

Unit 7 主题是"权利与规则"，常用于表达观点：

**表示同意（agree）**：
- I think teenagers **should be allowed to** choose their own clothes.
- It's important that students **be allowed to** express themselves.

**表示反对（disagree）**：
- I don't think teenagers **should be allowed to** drive.
- I believe children **shouldn't be allowed to** stay up too late.

### 对比：主动 vs 被动

| 主动 | 被动 |
|------|------|
| Parents should allow teenagers to choose their clothes. | Teenagers **should be allowed to** choose their clothes. |
| Teachers should not allow students to use phones. | Students **shouldn't be allowed to** use phones. |

### 中考考点

1. 结构：`should + be + allowed + to + 动词原形`（各成分齐全）。
2. 否定：`should not/shouldn't be allowed to`。
3. 疑问句：`Should + 主语 + be allowed to + 动词原形?`。
4. 与 Unit 7 话题（青少年权利）结合考查。
