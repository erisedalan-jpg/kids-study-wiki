# take out the trash

## 基本概念

**take out the trash** 意为"倒垃圾、扔垃圾"。美式英语用 **trash**（垃圾），英式英语也用 **rubbish** 或 **take out the bins**。

表达：**Could you take out the trash?** 你能倒垃圾吗？

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 核心用法

- Could you **take out the trash**? 你能倒垃圾吗？
- I have to **take out the trash** every night. 我每晚都要倒垃圾。
- He forgot to **take out the trash** again! 他又忘记倒垃圾了！

### 变体表达

| 表达 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| take out the trash | 美式，最常用 |
| take out the garbage | 美式，与 trash 同义 |
| take out the rubbish | 英式 |
| put out the bins | 英式口语 |

### 家务请求句型

- **Could you please** take out the trash? 你能倒垃圾吗？（礼貌请求）
- **Would you mind** taking out the trash? 你介意倒垃圾吗？（更礼貌）
- **Please** take out the trash. 请倒垃圾。（简单命令）

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### take out 短语的多义

**take out** 除了"倒垃圾"，还有其他含义：

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| take out the trash | 倒垃圾 | Take out the trash, please. |
| take sb out | 带某人外出（请客） | He took her out for dinner. |
| take out money | 取钱 | I need to take out some cash. |
| take out a book | 借书 | She took out a library book. |
| take out sth on sb | 拿某人出气 | Don't take it out on me! |

### 现在完成时

- **Have you taken out** the trash yet? 你倒垃圾了吗？
- I **haven't taken out** the trash yet. 我还没倒垃圾。
- She **has already taken out** the trash. 她已经倒了垃圾了。
