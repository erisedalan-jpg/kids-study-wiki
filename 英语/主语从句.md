## 🎓 主语从句

### 定义

**主语从句**：整个从句充当句子的**主语**。

- *[What she said] surprised me.* — 方括号内是主语
- *[That he won] is great news.*

### 两种书写形式

**形式 1：直接作主语（从句置于句首）**
- *[Whether he will come] is uncertain.*
- *[What you need] is more practice.*
- *[That she failed the exam] was a great shock.*

**形式 2：It 作形式主语（从句后置）—— 高考更常见**

| 原句（直接主语）| It 形式主语结构 |
|-------------|-------------|
| That he is honest is known to all. | **It** is known to all **that** he is honest. |
| Whether we go matters. | **It** matters **whether** we go. |
| What he wants is unclear. | **It** is unclear **what** he wants. |

> 形式主语 It 结构更常用，因为把长从句放句尾，语句更平衡（英语"重心后移"原则）

### 常见形式主语句型

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| It is + adj. + that... | It is important **that** we study hard. |
| It is + n. + that... | It is a fact **that** the earth is round. |
| It is said/reported/believed + that... | It is reported **that** 100 people survived. |
| It seems/appears + that... | It seems **that** she is unhappy. |
| It doesn't matter + whether/what... | It doesn't matter **whether** you agree. |

### 主语从句 vs 同位语从句

| 对比 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 主语从句（that 无实义，只引导）| **That** he passed surprised me. |
| 同位语从句（that 引导，解释前面名词）| The news **that** he passed surprised me. |

## 例句

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 她说的话让我惊讶。 | What she said surprised me. |
| 众所周知，他是诚实的。 | It is known to all that he is honest. |

| 据报道，有一百人幸存。 | It is reported that one hundred people survived. |
| 你努力学习是很重要的。 | It is important that you study hard. |
| 你是否同意并不重要。 | It doesn't matter whether you agree or not. |
