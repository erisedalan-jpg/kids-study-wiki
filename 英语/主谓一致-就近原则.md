# 主谓一致：就近原则

> **一句话**：就近原则指谓语动词与**最近的主语**在人称和数上保持一致，适用于特定连接词连接的并列主语结构。
> **English**: The proximity principle means the verb agrees with the nearest subject — applied when specific conjunctions connect compound subjects.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 适用就近原则的结构

| 结构 | 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| either...or... | 动词与 or 后的主语一致 | Either you or he **is** right. |
| neither...nor... | 动词与 nor 后的主语一致 | Neither the teacher nor the students **were** present. |
| not only...but also... | 动词与 also 后的主语一致 | Not only you but also she **has** made a mistake. |
| not...but... | 动词与 but 后的主语一致 | Not he but they **are** to blame. |
| there be / here be | 动词与最近（最先出现）的名词一致 | There **is** a pen and two books on the desk. |

### 与"就远原则"对比

| | 就近原则 | 就远原则 |
|--|---------|---------|
| 适用连词 | either...or / neither...nor / not only...but also | with / together with / as well as / along with / including |
| 动词与谁一致 | 离动词**最近**的主语 | 离动词**最远**（主句主语）|
| 例句 | Neither Tom nor I **am** going. | Tom, as well as his friends, **is** going. |

### 高考易错点

**错误示例（就近原则）**：
- ❌ Either the students or the teacher **are** responsible.
- ✅ Either the students or the teacher **is** responsible.（与 teacher 就近）

**there be 句型**：
- There **is** a book and two pens on the table.（与最近的 book 一致）
- There **are** two pens and a book on the table.（与最近的 pens 一致）

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 不是你就是他犯了错误。 | Either you or he **has** made a mistake. |
| 老师和学生们都没有出席。 | Neither the teacher nor the students **were** present. |
| 不仅他们，她也犯了错。 | Not only they but also she **has** made an error. |
| 桌上有一支钢笔和两本书。 | There **is** a pen and two books on the desk. |
| 不是他而是他们应受责备。 | Not he but they **are** to blame. |
| 不是金钱而是知识能改变命运。 | Not money but knowledge **is** able to change one's fate. |
