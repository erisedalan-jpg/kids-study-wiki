## 🎓 宾语从句（高中）

> 初中已学：[[宾语从句-初中]]（基础引导词、陈述语序）  
> 本词条：高中深化 — 时态呼应规则 + 虚拟语气 + 特殊结构

### 基本结构

**主语 + 及物动词 + [宾语从句]**

常见动词：know, think, believe, say, find, hope, wish, suggest, recommend, demand

### 引导词

| 引导词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|-------|------|------|
| **that** | 从句是陈述句；口语中可省略 | I think *(that)* he is right. |
| **whether/if** | 从句表是否；if 只用于宾语从句 | I wonder **whether/if** she will come. |
| **what/who/which/when/where/why/how** | 从句含疑问词 | Tell me **what** happened. |

### 时态呼应（高考核心）

| 主句时态 | 从句时态 | 例句 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 一般现在时 | 任意时态 | I know **he is / was / will be** here. |
| 一般过去时 | 过去时态（向后推移）| He said **he was** tired.（原句 he is tired）|
| 一般过去时 | 客观事实例外 | The teacher said the earth **is** round.（科学事实不变）|

**时态推移规则（主句过去时）**：

| 原时态 | 推移后 |
|-------|-------|
| is/am/are → | was/were |
| do/does → | did |
| will → | would |
| can → | could |
| has/have done → | had done |

### 宾语从句中的虚拟语气（高中重点）

某些动词后的 that 从句用虚拟语气 **(should) + 动词原形**：

**触发动词**：suggest, recommend, demand, require, insist, order, propose, advise

- *He suggested **that we (should) hold** a meeting.*
- *The doctor recommended **that she (should) rest** for a week.*
- *She insisted **that the window (should) be closed**.*

## 例句

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 我认为他是对的。 | I think (that) he is right. |
| 他说他很累。 | He said that he was tired. |
| 老师说地球是圆的。 | The teacher said the earth is round. |
| 我想知道她是否会来。 | I wonder whether/if she will come. |
| 请告诉我发生了什么。 | Please tell me what happened. |
| 他建议我们召开一次会议。 | He suggested that we (should) hold a meeting. |
