## 🎓 情态动词 could / would

**核心用法**：could 和 would 各有多重用法，是高中英语中最常考的情态动词之一。

---

### could 核心用法

| 用法 | 结构 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| can 的过去式 | could do | She could swim when she was five. |
| 礼貌请求 | Could you...? | Could you help me? |
| 委婉建议 | could do | You could try a different approach. |
| 可能性推测 | could be | That could be the answer. |
| 虚拟/未实现 | could have done | She could have won. |

### would 核心用法

| 用法 | 结构 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| will 的过去式 | would do | He said he would come. |
| 礼貌请求 | Would you...? | Would you mind closing the door? |
| 过去习惯 | would do | We would walk to school every day. |
| 虚拟条件 | would do | I would help if I could. |
| 委婉表达 | would like to | I would like to apply for the position. |

---

### 例句（中英对照）

| 英文例句 | 中文翻译 |
|----------|----------|
| Could you explain the body language to me? | 你能给我解释一下这个肢体语言吗？ |
| He could have been a great scientist if he had tried. | 如果他努力过，他本可以成为一位伟大的科学家。 |
| Would you like some more tea? | 你想再来些茶吗？ |
| She would always greet everyone with a warm smile. | 她总是以温暖的微笑问候每一个人。 |
| I would be grateful if you could review my application. | 如果您能审阅我的申请，我将不胜感激。 |
| That could be the reason why he was late. | 那可能就是他迟到的原因。 |

---

### 高考考点

1. **would like to vs would rather do**：
   - would like to do（想要做，礼貌）
   - would rather do（宁愿做，偏好）
   - would rather...than...（宁愿…也不愿）
2. **could have done**（虚拟/未实现）：对过去可能性的推测，含"但实际上没有"
3. **宾语从句中的时态**：主句过去时→从句情态动词用过去式（will→would, can→could）
4. **礼貌表达**：Would you mind doing...?（你介意做…吗？）

### 易错点

- **would do（过去习惯）vs used to do（过去习惯）**：两者均可，但 used to 强调与现在的对比
- **could have done**（本可以做，但没做）vs **should have done**（本应该做，但没做）
- Would you mind doing...? 注意回答：不介意 → No, not at all.（✓），介意 → I'm sorry, but...
