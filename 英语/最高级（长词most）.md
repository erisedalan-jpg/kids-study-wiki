# 最高级（长词 the most）

## 基本概念

多音节形容词的最高级用 **the most + 形容词** 构成，表示"最……"。

---

## 📚 6-12 岁自读版

### 句型结构

**the most + 形容词 + 名词 (+ 范围)**

- This is **the most interesting** book I've ever read. 这是我读过的最有趣的书。
- She is **the most popular** girl in school. 她是学校里最受欢迎的女生。

### 常见长词最高级

| 原级 | 最高级 |
|------|--------|
| outgoing | the most outgoing |
| interesting | the most interesting |
| important | the most important |
| beautiful | the most beautiful |
| difficult | the most difficult |
| expensive | the most expensive |
| popular | the most popular |

### 核心句型

**Which is the most + 形容词？**（哪个最……？）
- **Which** is **the most popular** subject in your school?
  哪门课是你们学校最受欢迎的？

---

## 🎓 12+ 进阶版

### least（最不……）

**the least + 形容词**是 the most 的反义：
- This is **the least difficult** question. 这是最不难的题目。（=最容易的）
- She is **the least outgoing** person in the group. 她是组里最不外向的人。

### 不规则形式

| 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级 |
|------|--------|--------|
| good | better | **the best** |
| bad | worse | **the worst** |
| many/much | more | **the most** |
| little | less | **the least** |

### 句型扩展

1. **Which is the most + 形容词, A, B or C?**
   - **Which** is **the most exciting**, basketball, soccer or tennis?

2. **one of the most + 形容词 + 复数名词**
   - The Great Wall is **one of the most famous** landmarks in the world.

---
