# 环保主题词汇

> **一句话**：环保主题词汇是高考英语完形填空、阅读理解和书面表达中高频出现的、围绕环境保护、可持续发展、污染治理等话题的核心词群，掌握其拼写、搭配与语境用法是突破环保类题型的词汇基础。
> **English**: Environmental protection vocabulary refers to the set of core words and phrases frequently appearing in Gaokao English tasks—covering topics such as pollution, sustainability, and conservation—whose correct spelling, collocation, and contextual usage are essential for tackling environment-themed questions.

## 🎓 考点精讲

**1. 核心词汇分类**

高考环保主题词汇按语义场可分为四类：

- **污染与破坏**：pollution, pollutant, contaminate, emission, exhaust, toxic, deforestation, carbon dioxide (CO₂), greenhouse effect, global warming, acid rain, smog, haze, particulate matter (PM2.5), landfill, waste disposal
- **保护与治理**：conservation, preservation, sustainable, renewable, biodegradable, recycle, reuse, reduce, restore, purify, filter, sewage treatment, afforestation, carbon neutral
- **生态与物种**：ecosystem, biodiversity, habitat, species, endangered, extinct, wildlife, vegetation, wetland, rainforest, food chain, ecological balance
- **能源与行动**：solar energy, wind power, hydroelectric, fossil fuel, alternative energy, low-carbon, energy-efficient, environmental awareness, take action, call for, advocate

**2. 高考考查形式与解题关键**

- **完形填空**：考查词汇在环保语境中的近义辨析与固定搭配。例如：区分 `conserve`（合理利用保护）与 `preserve`（保持原状保护）；判断 `reduce / reuse / recycle` 的 "3R" 顺序对应关系；识别 `take measures / steps / action to do sth.` 的固定搭配。
- **阅读理解**：环保类说明文或议论文中，要求根据上下文推断词义或理解作者态度。关键技巧是识别"问题—原因—解决方案"的篇章结构，借助同义复现（如 `carbon emissions = greenhouse gas emissions`）和反义对比（如 `renewable vs. fossil fuel`）定位答案。
- **书面表达**：环保话题写作（倡议书、演讲稿、建议信）要求准确使用主题词汇并实现搭配升级。基础表达如 `protect the environment` 应替换为 `raise environmental awareness` 或 `promote sustainable development`；`pollute the air` 可升级为 `release toxic emissions into the atmosphere`。

**3. 典型设问场景**

- 完形填空：一篇关于 plastic pollution 的短文，空格处需填入 `biodegradable`（可生物降解的）或 `dispose of`（处理）。
- 书面表达：题干要求写一封倡议信，呼吁同学参与 "Low-carbon Campus" 活动，需用到 `carbon footprint, energy-efficient, switch off, reusable` 等词。

## ⚠️ 高频易错点

- **形容词与名词混淆**：将 `environment`（名词，环境）误用作定语，正确形容词形式是 `environmental`（如 environmental problems）。同样，`pollution` 是名词，动词为 `pollute`，形容词 `polluted`（被污染的）。
- **conserve vs. preserve 辨析**：`conserve` 强调"节约使用、合理利用"（conserve water / energy），`preserve` 强调"保护原貌、防止破坏"（preserve the forest / wildlife）。完形填空中常根据宾语是资源类还是生态类来区分。
- **sustainable 拼写错误**：高频误拼为 `sustainible` 或 `sustanable`，正确拼写为 `sus-tain-able`，词根 `sustain` + 形容词后缀 `-able`。
- **emission 单复数与介词搭配**：表示"排放物"常用复数 `emissions`（carbon emissions），搭配介词 `of` 或 `from`（the emission of greenhouse gases / emissions from factories）。
