# 现在完成进行时（深化）

> **一句话**：现在完成进行时（have/has been doing）强调动作从过去某时开始，持续至今，且可能继续，与已有词条[[现在完成进行时]]互补，本篇聚焦高中深化用法与高考考点。
> **English**: The Present Perfect Progressive (have/has been doing) emphasizes an action that started in the past and is still continuing or has just stopped, with a focus on the duration and ongoing nature.

---

## 🎓 规则·例句对照·高考考点

### 构成

| 人称 | 构成 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| I / You / We / They | have been + 动词-ing | I have been studying for 3 hours. |
| He / She / It | has been + 动词-ing | She has been waiting since 9 o'clock. |

### 核心用法辨析

| 用法 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 强调持续时间 | 常与 for/since 连用，强调"一直" | He has been working here for 10 years. |
| 强调动作仍在进行 | 说话时动作尚未结束 | The baby has been crying for an hour. |
| 说明原因（动作刚停） | 解释当前状态的原因 | I'm tired because I have been running. |
| 表达抱怨/不满（口语） | 语气含批评意味 | He has been making noise all morning! |

### 与现在完成时对比（高考重点）

| 对比点 | 现在完成进行时 | 现在完成时 |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| 侧重 | 动作的**持续过程** | 动作的**完成结果** |
| 例句 | I have been reading the book.（还在读） | I have read the book.（读完了） |
| 常用状语 | for two hours / since morning / all day | already / yet / just / ever / never |
| 是否可继续 | 动作通常仍在持续或刚结束 | 动作已经完成 |

### 不能用完成进行时的动词（状态动词）

状态动词不能用进行时态：know / believe / understand / belong / like / love / hate / want / seem / contain

错误：❌ I have been knowing him for years.
正确：✅ I have known him for years.

### 高考考点

| 题型 | 考查方式 | 例题 |
|------|---------|------|
| 语法填空 | 根据语境填写正确时态 | He _____ (work) on the project since last month. → has been working |
| 完形填空 | 选择正确时态选项 | 区分 have done / have been doing |
| 书面表达 | 正确运用时态描述持续活动 | The factory has been polluting the river for decades. |

### 中英例句对照

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 科学家们已经研究这个问题很多年了。 | Scientists have been studying this problem for many years. |
| 他一直在等你，等了将近一个小时了。 | He has been waiting for you for almost an hour. |
| 他们自搬进新家以来，一直在装修房子。 | They have been decorating the house since they moved in. |
| 她累了，因为她一整天都在练钢琴。 | She is tired because she has been practising the piano all day. |
| 工厂已经排污数十年，严重破坏了生态环境。 | The factory has been discharging waste for decades, severely damaging the ecosystem. |
| 气候变化问题已经困扰人类社会多年。 | The issue of climate change has been troubling human society for many years. |
