# 外国文学

> **一句话**：外国文学指**中国之外其他国家、地区、民族的文学作品**——从古希腊神话到当代欧美小说，是与中国文学对话的"他山之石"，也是建立世界视野的重要途径。
> **English**: *Foreign literature* refers to literary works from countries, regions, and peoples outside China — from ancient Greek myths to contemporary Western novels; "stones from other mountains" that broaden the worldview alongside Chinese literature.
